# Independent frontier search lanes

The founder profile must not be included in scout prompts beyond “technically fluent hardware
founder.” Each lane targets 45–55 collected sources and at least 40 accepted after checks.

1. L01 Industrial plasma, plasma chemistry, and plasma power supplies.
2. L02 Extreme-efficiency and ultra-high-density power electronics.
3. L03 Superconducting systems beyond sensing: magnets, machines, current leads, cryogenics.
4. L04 Advanced generation and heat-to-power: sCO2, turbomachinery controls, thermophotovoltaics,
   high-temperature conversion, and balance-of-plant bottlenecks.
5. L05 Pulsed power, RF/microwave, electron/ion-beam, and accelerator subsystems.
6. L06 Semiconductor manufacturing plasma/RF/high-voltage subsystem niches.
7. L07 Vacuum, cryogenic, leak-tight, and ultra-clean process hardware.
8. L08 DC grids, grid-forming hardware, protection, and high-power interconnects.
9. L09 Space electric power, electric propulsion support, and radiation-tolerant power/control.
10. L10 Maritime, mining, rail, and off-highway electrification.
11. L11 Electrochemical manufacturing, hydrogen, e-fuels, and precision power delivery.
12. L12 Photonics, high-power lasers, beam control, and optical manufacturing subsystems.
13. L13 Quantum/big-science classical electronics, timing, control, and facility hardware.
14. L14 Extreme thermal management and two-phase cooling for power-dense systems.
15. L15 Harsh-environment/high-temperature electronics and autonomous industrial control.
16. L16 Wildcards: electrohydrodynamics, metamaterial hardware, acoustic/electromagnetic process
    tools, advanced motors/generators, and other evidence-backed frontier niches missed above.

Independence rule: scouts may report cross-links but cannot discard a lane because a different
lane appears more familiar. The Fable architect combines lanes only after all 16 maps exist.
