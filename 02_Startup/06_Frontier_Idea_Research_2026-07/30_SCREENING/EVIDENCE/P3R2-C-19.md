# P3R2-C-19 — Demand/Competitor Evidence

Concept: Gas-foil bearing and shaft-seal cartridges for sCO2/ORC turbomachinery — standardized
rotor-support modules. Primary market: US+CN (dual). **Binding pre-promotion conditions
(`fix_applied_notes`)**: (1) structural pairing with **P3R2-C-08** (PCHE recuperators) as one
high-temperature-components company — evaluated jointly with the C-08 evidence file; (2) a 2028

## 1. Demand

Demand triggers for this idea are the same underlying US/CN sCO2 commercialization wave documented
in the paired `P3R2-C-08` evidence file (STEP Demo operational status, NRC Part 53, Chaotan One
operational status, CNNC Xinjiang 2028 project) — not re-derived in full here to avoid duplication;
see `30_SCREENING/EVIDENCE/P3R2-C-08.md` Section 1 for the full citations. The idea-specific question
for C-19 is narrower: **has the 2028 machine-build-signal gate (STEP Phase 2 hardware orders, or
CNNC first-set follow-on orders) been triggered yet?**

- **STEP Phase 2 status, checked directly this session**: STEP Demo Phase 1 testing was completed
  (announced 2024-10-07; reused atlas `L04-039`) and the plant was confirmed operational as of March
  2026 (search-confirmed, SwRI site update). **No announcement of a "Phase 2" commercial hardware
  order or follow-on commercial unit was found this session** — searches for "STEP Demo Phase 2
  commercial sCO2 order" returned only Phase 1 completion coverage and GE Vernova's routine Q1 2026
  8-K filings with no sCO2-specific commercial order disclosed. **The gate has not yet been
  triggered on the US side as of this session's research (mid-2026)** — consistent with the seed's
  own conservative "hold as an option until a machine-build signal" instruction; this is not a
  negative finding on demand, but confirms the seed's own precompany plan should not yet advance past
  test-cell work on the US trigger.
- **CNNC first-set follow-on status**: Chaotan One (Guizhou) is now fully operational (both units
  online as of 2026-05-30, per the paired C-08 evidence file) — this is arguably a stronger, already-
  triggered CN-side signal than a future "follow-on order," since the first-set unit itself is
  running commercially. Whether Chaotan One's own rotor-support components were sourced from a
  merchant cartridge vendor or built in-house was not established this session (no vendor/BOM
  disclosure found) — a genuine research gap, not confirmed either way.

**Verdict: demand exists as a derivative of the paired C-08 sCO2 buildout (real, per the C-08
file), but the idea-specific 2028 gate for accelerating capex is honestly not yet triggered on the
clearest US metric (STEP Phase 2 hardware orders) as of mid-2026 — the seed's own staged, option-
like capital discipline is the correct posture right now, not a signal to proceed to full-skid
investment.**

## 2. Competitors — seal-major incumbent mapping (binding P4 requirement)

- **John Crane — confirmed as an active, named, already-shipping commercial sCO2 seal vendor,
  not a hypothetical future entrant.** Direct fetch (`P3R2-C-19-S01`, johncrane.com) confirms: "Our
  sealing solutions were selected for the turbomachinery in the world's first Allam Cycle power
  plant, using sCO2 as the working fluid," with a named product portfolio already marketed for sCO2
  service — Aura 220 dry gas seals, Type 28XP/28EXP compressor seals, separation seals, gas seal
  support systems — and a claim of "nearly three decades of supporting carbon capture initiatives."
  John Crane was also separately selected by Franco Tosi Meccanica for the SOLARSCO2OL sCO2 pilot
  project (search-confirmed, John Crane press material, not independently re-fetched). **This is a
  materially adverse finding for the seed's differentiation claim**: the seed's own `competition_
  outlook_2030` field frames seal majors as "industry names, not in atlas — flag for P4" and
  implicitly treats them as not-yet-engaged; in fact John Crane is **already an in-market, named,
  multi-project sCO2 seal incumbent today**, not a future entrant to watch for. This is a stronger
  incumbent-response signal than the seed anticipated.
- **EagleBurgmann** — no sCO2-specific product or project reference was found this session (search
  only); absence of evidence is not evidence of absence, but no confirmation of EagleBurgmann's
  sCO2 activity was obtained, unlike the clear John Crane confirmation.
- **China**: SMDERI and CAS-IET institute-affiliated compressor programs (reused atlas
  `L04-102/106/109`) remain the seed's cited CN-side rotor-support developers; not independently
  re-verified this session beyond the existing atlas citations.

**Competitor-map conclusion (satisfies the binding P4 gate, with an adverse result): John Crane is a
real, active, multi-project incumbent already selling dry-gas seals into commercial and pilot sCO2
turbomachinery, including the flagship Allam Cycle plant. This narrows the seed's addressable

no equivalent commercial incumbent was found this session) than on the seal side, where a global
seal major is already qualified and shipping.** This should be reflected in scoring: the "shaft-seal"
half of this idea's product bundle faces a stronger incumbent than the "gas-foil-bearing" half.
