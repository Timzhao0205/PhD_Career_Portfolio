  summary, hklaw.com, fetched) — an interim final rule published September 30, 2025 extends
  Entity-List/MEU-List restrictions to foreign affiliates owned, directly or indirectly, 50%+ by one
  or more listed parties, with aggregation across multiple listed owners.
- **Ownership-chain question on the idea's own named CN example (CGN Dasheng) — discovery-lead,
  WebSearch only, NOT independently fetched or confirmed this session:** search results describing
  CGN Tech's (000881) equity structure suggest its direct controlling shareholder holds only ~27.28%

  controller" (实际控制人) status is a distinct legal concept from EAR ownership-percentage tests, and
  the rule aggregates direct+indirect ownership across the full chain, which this session's research
  did not trace with primary-source confidence. **This is reported as an open, unresolved compliance
  question, not a cleared or blocked finding — recommend export-counsel review of the complete
  ownership chain before any CGN Dasheng-adjacent engagement.**

  channel (CAS/big-science tenders) is domestic-manufacturer-only by rule.** This substantially
