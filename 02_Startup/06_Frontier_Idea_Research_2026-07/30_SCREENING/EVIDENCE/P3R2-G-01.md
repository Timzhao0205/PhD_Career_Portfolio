## 1. Demand

**Primary buyer/procurement anchor (atlas):** L07-045 -- IHEP's 2021 HEPS storage-ring vacuum-chamber tender (ccnta.cn),
China-registered-manufacturer-only eligibility clause independently re-verified at the primary document by the seed's
own P4 process. This anchor is aging (2021-vintage), which is exactly why the judge imposed the multi-tender-cadence
binding condition.

**Multi-tender cadence beyond CEPC (binding condition -- SATISFIED):** ASIPP's **BEST** facility (紧凑型聚变能实验装置,
Compact Fusion Energy Experimental Device, Hefei) is an active, independently-funded (RMB 8.5B total investment)
facility distinct from CEPC/HEPS, under construction since May 2025 and targeting a 2030 net-fusion-power-gain
demonstration (per state-media reporting, **P3R2-G-01-S04**). Hesuan Zhineng / 合锻智能 (603011.SH), a listed A-share
fabricator, won a **RMB 209 million** tender in 2024 to manufacture BEST's vacuum-chamber segments 1-4, delivering to
welding roundness <=1mm and machining precision +/-0.05mm, explicitly described as "ITER同级标准" (ITER-equivalent
standard) (**P3R2-G-01-S02**). Secondary reporting (not independently re-fetched to primary standard this session)
indicates a further ~RMB 500M supplementary BEST order signed with CNNC and a December-2025 shielding-blanket
stainless-steel-shield-block tender bid, for cumulative fusion-related orders exceeding RMB 700M at this one fabricator
-- flagged as a corroborating lead, not a formally re-verified figure. **This satisfies the binding condition:** CEPC,
HEPS, and BEST/ASIPP represent at least three distinct CN big-science vacuum-hardware procurement programs, with BEST
providing live, dated, large-value (RMB 209M+) tender activity independent of CEPC's fate.

**Fabricator-owns-acceptance 2028 evidence gate (binding condition -- PARTIALLY SATISFIED):** 合锻智能's own disclosed
delivery specification (roundness, machining precision to ITER-class tolerance) is itself a form of fabricator-facing
acceptance criterion imposed by the procuring institute, though it is geometric/mechanical rather than vacuum-specific
(hydrogen-outgassing/bakeout-endpoint). Corroborating leads found via search (not independently re-fetched this session
due to a persistent TLS certificate error on ipp.cas.cn/ipp.ac.cn encountered on repeated WebFetch attempts) indicate
ASIPP's own tender documents for comparable vacuum chambers (EAST-NBI, CFETR-NNBI classes) specify explicit ultimate-
vacuum and total-leak-rate acceptance thresholds for externally-fabricated hardware. Combined, this is directionally
strong support for "fabricators, not institutes, own conditioning/acceptance" -- but a hydrogen-outgassing/bakeout-
specific acceptance clause (as opposed to geometric or leak-rate) was not independently confirmed via direct document
fetch this session. **Recommend treating this gate as provisionally met pending direct tender-document access**, not
fully closed.

## 4. Certification and export constraints

**China National Institute of Metrology (NIM) anchor (binding condition -- SATISFIED):** NIM operates an official
national "比较法真空标准装置" (comparison-method vacuum standard device), certificate **[2010]国量标计证字第225号**,

standard and lead authorship of China's national vacuum-gauge calibration standard. This satisfies the "name a
NIM-class CN metrology anchor for witness-standard traceability" requirement.

## 5. US / CN verdict

on CEPC.** CEPC was **not** recommended by the Chinese Academy of Sciences for inclusion in China's 15th Five-Year Plan

chamber procurement) means the CN leg's demand case no longer rests on CEPC alone -- exactly the diversification the
binding P4 condition was designed to force. Net: the underlying CN big-science vacuum-acceptance demand is real and
already transacting NOW (2024-2026), independent of CEPC's now-more-uncertain timeline.
