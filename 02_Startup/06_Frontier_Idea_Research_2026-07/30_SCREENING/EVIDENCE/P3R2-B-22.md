# P3R2-B-22 — Demand/Competitor Evidence

Concept: DC-link capacitor and converter health-prognostics retrofit module — embedded ripple/ESR/
thermal sensing with physics-based remaining-useful-life models — for Chinese HVDC datacenter and
industrial converter fleets. Primary market: CN (US kept as active secondary).

**Binding pre-promotion condition (fix_applied_notes, 2026-07-13 FIX):** "LOI from one named
design-win path — a PSU/UPS vendor or a third-party maintenance operator; absent it, treat as a
feature (sell/license the IP) rather than a company." Evaluated first because it governs the gate
result.

## 1. Demand

**Searched for:** a named LOI or design-win with a PSU/UPS vendor or third-party maintenance
operator for capacitor/converter health-prognostics retrofit hardware.

**Found:** no LOI, design-win announcement, or pilot-customer commitment naming this specific
product category (capacitor ESR/RUL prognostics retrofit module) was located in English- or
Chinese-language search this session, for any vendor.

**Adjacent, non-satisfying evidence found:**
- China's domestic HVDC datacenter power market is real, dated, and growing: ~RMB 10B in 2026 at an
  estimated 10-15% penetration of new AIDC builds, brokerage-report forecast of >RMB 80B domestic
  HVDC market by 2030 (Chinese securities-research PDF aggregation, search-only — **T3, not
  independently fetched, treat as directional trade-press/brokerage estimate, not a primary
  source**).
- Named incumbent HVDC/UPS vendors with existing telemetry/predictive-maintenance features already
  in-market: Zhongheng Electric (中恒电气, >50% share of China's datacenter HVDC power-system
  market per trade reporting), Delta China, Kstar (科士达), Kehua Data (科华数据) — all five locked
  in as Alibaba's "Panama power" HVDC suppliers per trade reporting (search-only synthesis, not
  independently fetched this session). Kehua Data's own marketing explicitly claims integrated
  "digitalized monitoring, predictive maintenance" features in its UPS/HVDC lines (search-only,
  vendor claim) — **this is evidence competitors already bundle basic predictive-maintenance
  telemetry, not evidence of unmet demand for a specialized third-party retrofit module.**
- SuperX Digital Power Pte Ltd. (JV with Zhongheng Electric) launched two 800VDC products,
  "Panama-800VDC" (new-build, claimed 98.5% peak efficiency) and "Aurora-800VDC" (retrofit,
  hours-per-cabinet install), 2025-11-02 (`P3R2-B-22-S01`, stcn.com, fetched). This confirms an
  active retrofit market exists for 800V power hardware in China, and names a real vendor (SuperX/
  Zhongheng) that could plausibly be a design-win target for a prognostics add-on — but no
  prognostics-specific co-development or LOI with this or any other vendor was found.

**Verdict: the FIX gate is NOT satisfied.** No named LOI or design-win with a PSU/UPS vendor or
maintenance operator was found. Per the seed's own fallback framing, this should currently be
treated as a feature/IP-licensing opportunity rather than a standalone company case.

  Zhongheng half of the SuperX JV (`P3R2-B-22-S01`). In-house telemetry is the default competitive
  response the seed itself anticipates.
- Delta China, Kstar (科士达), Kehua Data (科华数据) — the other three of five vendors supplying
  Alibaba's "Panama power" HVDC architecture (reused atlas context `L02-048`); Kehua markets
  predictive-maintenance features as standard, per vendor marketing (not independently verified).
- No dedicated third-party capacitor-health-prognostics startup (China, US, or elsewhere) was
  identified in this session's searches; the competitive risk is vendor in-house telemetry rather
  than a funded prognostics-specific rival.
