## 1. Landscape read

**Two disjoint filing worlds; the seam between them is where P08 lives.**

**(a) Generic module paralleling (datacenter/telecom/industrial): dense, old, largely spent.** Droop-style current equalization was claimed by 1989 [US4924170A, Unisys]; master-election share controllers with hot-swap by 2000 [US6236582B1, Micro Linear]; dual-grid hot-swappable N+1 bulk AC-DC systems by 2000 [US6356470B1, HP]. All display as expired-class (indicative — verify before reliance). The live tail is narrow circuit work: Delta's coupled-inductor loop-current limiting (2016 priority, runs to ~2037) [US9960699B2], Liebert/Vertiv's paired-inductor UPS bus sharing (2015) [US10404094B2], Silergy's CN-priority multi-stage auxiliary-module circuit (2020) [US11817781B2]. Charter question answered: generic N+1 art already covers voltage-bus redundancy, droop sharing, master/slave election, hot-swap insertion, and loop-current suppression — a P08 entrant cannot claim those mechanisms as such against 25+ years of art, but equally none of it claims precision current-mode sharing into a single high-inductance superconducting load, ppm-class share accuracy on a DCCT reference, magnet-safe module extraction (freewheel continuity), or per-coil array drive.

**(b) Magnet-side paralleling/orchestration: sparse, institutional, aging.** ESRF claimed per-magnet dedicated converters with an automatically-switched standby unit in 2015 — the closest N+1-for-magnet-arrays claim found; the FR member displays lapsed, US/EP members exist [FR3042323B1; US9743506B2]. CERN/EPFL's storage-integrated series DC/DC (POPS lineage, 2006) displays expired [US8723365B2]. Hitachi claimed shared-ramp-pattern plus per-magnet corrective-pattern distribution to many synchrotron supplies in 2011 (family expiry ~2031, indicative) [WO2012117563A1]. Bechtel/Siemens combined modular parallel AC/DC converters with linkable SC magnets in 1998 [US6215202B1]. Pattern matches U-018/U-032: labs filed the concepts once, let much of it lapse, and never productized.

**(c) The fusion-era refill (2018-2026) — where the live fences are.**
- **Thea Energy seam (charter mandate).** The Princeton/PPPL planar-coil-stellarator patent Thea licenses (priority 2022-03) is granted with dependent claims 28-32 reciting controllers, power supplies adjusting current in coil subsets, and switching systems connecting supplies to subsets [US12100520B2]. Thea's OWN granted filing (priority 2023-04) claims removable field-shaping-unit mechanics; controllers appear only generically and no converter, per-coil supply, or protection-electronics claims appear in the published family [US12451260B2]. Mandate verdict: shaping-by-software is fenced at system level (geometry plus supplies+switching recitations), not at converter level. Pending continuations can still grow claims — re-check quarterly.
- **Tokamak Energy** holds in-cryostat per-magnet SMPS claims with ramp/steady-state mode control (2018) [US11193996B2] — consistent with its captive converter+magnet bundling posture (CS-H).
- **HFIPS/ASIPP** took a 2026-02 US grant on an integrated fusion magnet + power supply + tri-modal quench-diagnostic system (CN priority 2023-08) [US12555710B2] — a CN institute fencing the converter+protection integration seam in the US market.
- **IMP (HIAF fast-ramp mandate).** Full-storage pulsed-supply architecture (storage caps deliver all ramp energy, recover fall energy; modular H-bridge units in series/parallel), 2018: the invention application was REJECTED in 2025 [CN108134534A]; the same-day utility model is ACTIVE and expires 2028-02-05 [CN207835363U]. The CN fence on storage-ramp modular supplies is a utility model that dies before a 2029/2030 launch (displays active — verify).
- **SJTU** holds a CN-priority US grant on capacitive pulsed-current charging reaching kA-class magnetization from a watt-class source (2020) [US11871683B2] — an alternative kA-scaling route adjacent to U-014 economics.
- **Actionpower / XJ leads:** resolved in §4 — Actionpower's reconfiguration/coordination family is now verified [CN115453402B; CN121433747A] plus two new magnet-specific 2026 publications [CN121785095A; CN121772129A]; the XJ pulse-storage grant displays lapsed [CN108335923B]. The 300 MVA supercap-cascade TF-supply filing expected from CS-G did NOT surface under any 许继 assignee variant searched (许继电气/许继集团/许继电源/河南许继电力电子/西安许继电力电子; re-swept in §4) — bounded absence, news-level evidence only [CS-G-07].

## 3. Whitespace hypotheses (bounded absences — hypotheses only)
