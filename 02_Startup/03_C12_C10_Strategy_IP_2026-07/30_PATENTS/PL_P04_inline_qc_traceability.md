## 1. Landscape read

**Assignee × filing-year picture.** Three generations. (a) 1997–2010, institute tape metrology: Forschungszentrum Karlsruhe's contactless Hall-array Jc scanner [US6034527A] (the TapeStar-class foundation), UC/LANL's localized-field Ic rig [US6452375B1], CEPRI/State Grid's Ic-vs-(T,B,θ) method [CN101446612B], SuperPower's position-correlated tape dataset [US7854057B2], KERI's wheel-contact continuous-Ic bench [US8441247B2]. (b) 2011–2018, vendor/OEM consolidation of tape QC and operational protection: NIMS/JASTEC screening-field derating [US9543754B2], Univ. Houston in-growth QC (microwave [US12369500B2], in-line XRD [EP3507840B1]), Bruker in-field reel-to-reel Ic [EP3671247A1], NCSU fiber-in-tape [US10892397B2], CAS-IEE quench detection/protection [CN101126787B][CN102214911B]. (c) 2016–2020 priorities, fusion privates fencing coil-level MONITORING and manufacture: Tokamak Energy with four distinct families [US11101059B2][US11776721B2][US11749434B2][CN115485868A] — the densest assignee of the cluster — and CFS holding the BASF/Jülich-lineage reel-to-reel Ic QC apparatus [US12050254B2] (JP sibling JP7186243B2 confirmed same family). Canon Medical's during-winding position-control grant [US12040130B2] is the only 2020s document touching the winding stage itself.

**Dense vs empty.** Dense: (i) reel-to-reel tape Ic measurement — contact (CFS, KERI, Bruker), contactless (FZK/KIT), in-growth (Houston ×2); (ii) operational quench detection/protection of finished magnets (TE ×3, CAS-IEE ×2, NIMS). Empty across all five passes: any electrical/defect QC claim exercised DURING winding; any winding controller consuming a tape defect map; any coil-level post-wind QC/certification instrument; any superconductor-specific manufacturing-traceability claim. The charter mandate is answered: **no fetched document claims during-winding Rc or defect QC** — the closest are geometric placement control [US12040130B2] and pre-winding reel-to-reel electrical QC [US12050254B2].

**CN vs US center of gravity.** Instrument art sits US/EP/JP/KR (CFS, KERI, Bruker, Houston, Canon). Domestic CN filings found are institute method/protection art that lapses fast for fees ([CN101126787B] lapsed 2016; [CN102214911B] lapsed 2025; the only tension+Ic bench, utility model [CN206348380U], lapsed after two years). Meanwhile Western fusion filers are nationalizing INTO CN: TE's [CN115485868A] (granted 2026-04 as displayed) and CN114667579B in the [US11749434B2] family. Zh sweeps for 上海超导, 星环聚能, 能量奇点, ASIPP on winding/coil QC returned nothing on-point (bounding note: a 2026 Startorus quench-detection application — coupling coil + bridge + comparator — is press-reported, but the reported number 404s on Google Patents and is therefore not citable this run). Net: CN is a market being fenced by Western filers, not yet a domestic filing wave in this niche — a filing window relevant to Rule 6.
