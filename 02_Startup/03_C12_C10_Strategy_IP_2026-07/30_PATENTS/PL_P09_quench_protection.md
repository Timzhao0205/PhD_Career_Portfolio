Systematic modality fencing that matches CS-H: TE monetizes protection only inside magnet sales; the estate deters imitation rather than serving third-party coil builders (U-025).

**FSU/NHMFL is the second pillar**, instrument-adjacent but coil-invasive: embedded distributed heaters (US7649720B2, priority 2005 — oldest HTS-specific blocking art; its 20-year term runs out ~2025-26, *indicative, verify*), NI charging-delay feedback via PSU control (US11094438B2), and the CLIQ-analog frequency-loss injection (US11217373B2). CERN holds the LTS-era CLIQ grant (EP3014634B1, EP-only, to 2034).

**CFS files magnets, not protection electronics.** Five separate passes surfaced **no** QD/QPS electronics filing assigned to Commonwealth Fusion Systems; the protection posture lives in passive NI magnet-architecture families (MIT-assigned lineage), and MIT's one true detection grant is the pressure-wave sensor-array method (US12050139B2, 2024). Bounded absence: passes run 2026-07-04, 18-month publication lag applies.

**MRI/industrial incumbents form an aging background layer:** Siemens external-dump switching (US8345392B2, 2010), GE's China-origin M-of-N heater matrix (US10586638B2, CN priority 2014), Mitsubishi Heavy Industries' bridge-circuit QD with hold-period noise rejection (US8405410B2, JP priority 2008 — the canonical bridge+comparator art), Oxford Instruments' movable heater (GB2514372A) and varistor-triggered QPS (US12112886B2, active to 2041), IEE-CAS heater QPS (CN102214911B — displays *Expired-Fee-Related*), HUST thermal-shield energy transfer filed into the US on a CN priority (US11869709B2). **CN vs US center of gravity is split by layer.** Detection/method IP is UK/US-weighted (TE, MIT, FSU). Dump **switchgear** IP is CN-weighted: NJIT's three-stage hybrid QPS switch (CN111541222A/B, 2020) sits inside a live 2020-2023 CN citation cluster of fusion-specific explosion (pyro) breakers and hybrid DC switches feeding EAST/CFETR/BEST programs — now fetch-verified (top-up section): NJIT's own follow-on hybrid switch (CN112736841A), HFIPS's RDX pyrobreaker to 2041 (CN113097026B) and water-cooled 50 kA QPS breaker to 2042 (CN114551187B) — consistent with ASIPP tendering a "定型产品" 100 kA QPS switch because no product exists (U-024).
