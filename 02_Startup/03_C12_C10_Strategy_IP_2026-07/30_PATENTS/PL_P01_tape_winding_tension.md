## 1. Landscape read

**Assignee × filing-year.** Densest filer: **CAS institutes (5 families)** — IEE's stepped-shaft double-pancake fixture (CN1707708B, 2004 priority, displays expired-fee-related), HFIPS's PF dual-line winding system (CN108597850A, 2018) and interlayer-transition forming machine filed into the US (US11581133B2, 2019, displays active to 2041), then IEE's 2022 pair (jointless multi-pancake machine CN114388259B; closed-loop-magnet winding method CN114360894B). Around CAS sits a CN apparatus wave, 2017-2023: Guangdong Power Grid EPRI's feedback-tension double-tape winder (CN107481854B, 2017), Xi'an Juneng's DP method + jointless shaping device (CN108461275B 2018, CN112951584B 2021), Zhongtian's multi-reel servo-tension equipment (CN110271911B, 2019), and the Hefei ecosystem's pending co-wind devices (Xihe CN114678207A 2022 — transferred from the Anhui energy-lab incubator, matching CS-B card 3; Hefei Nuclear Polymagnet CN115621033A 2022), plus Tsinghua's fiber strain monitor (CN115615347B, 2022). The West files coil *constructions and materials*, not machines: MIT 2012 (NI multi-width US9117578B2; twist-wind US9105396B2), FSU 2022 (Rc-by-coatings US20230238178A1, pending), Tokamak Energy 2018-2021 (shunt placement US11289253B2; tape-pair alignment CA3111888A1 with granted CN112955975B member; shingle winding WO2023083956A1 with EP4430643B1 granted 2025 and CN118235218A pending), CFS 2018 (tape QC US20210208217A1, granted US12050254B2, CN112074750B granted). Korea holds the machine-metrology edge: KERI's dual-tension co-winder (KR101597685B1, 2014) and SuperGenics' laser-sensed alignment-guide winding system (KR102825078B1, priority 2023-12, granted 2025-06). Japan: Toshiba non-circular winding (JP6168751B2, 2012), Canon Medical during-winding camera inspection (JP7361613B2, 2020), Nittoku generic back-tension (JP5623794B2, displays lapsed 2020).

**Dense vs empty.** Dense: constant-tension apparatus claims (magnetic-powder, damper/weight, servo 0-200 N), co-winding/MI parallel feed, jointless multi-pancake sequencing, DP fixtures — mostly CN, H01F41/048+41/094. Empty across all passes: programmed tension *profiles*, any tension→Rc linkage, defect-map-driven feed, camber/twist metrology, winding-data traceability (see §4). Also empty: the Western machine vendors themselves — assignee sweeps for Ridgway Machines, Broomfield, Supertek, Winding Services Ltd returned no SC-winding filings, and Aumann/Marsilli surfaced nothing SC-specific (Nittoku's dense copper-winding estate, e.g. JP5623794B2, is the generic prior-art floor CS-A card 5 warned about). Sweeps for 上海超导科技股份有限公司, 能量奇点能源科技, 星环聚能/Startorus, 原力辰, 聚能电物理 surfaced no winding-machine families under the names searched — the CN integrators' "process = moat" posture (CS-B) shows up as trade secrecy, not filings; the exception is 曦合 via its incubator-transferred CN114678207A.

**CN vs US center of gravity.** Machine/apparatus gravity is squarely CN: 12 of 25 fetched records are CN documents, and 3 more families carry granted/pending CN members (CN112955975B, CN112074750B, CN118235218A). US-jurisdiction records are architecture/method/QC (MIT, FSU, TE, CFS) plus one CAS machine filed outbound. Placement metrology art is KR/JP, not CN or US — a striking split: China patents tension hardware, Korea/Japan patent guidance and inspection, the US patents coil physics.

**Expiry horizon (indicative only).** First-generation CN fixture art already displays as lapsed (CN1707708B, fee-related 2016; Nittoku JP5623794B2 lapsed 2020) — displays as expired, verify before reliance. The MIT 2012 pair displays active to 2033, KERI to 2034; the CN winder core (2017-2019 priorities) runs to ~2037-2039; the 2022-2023 wave (CAS IEE, Xihe, Tsinghua, SuperGenics) to ~2042-2045. Practical read: nothing blocking-relevant ages out before ~2033, and the tension/placement apparatus space stays encumbered in CN into the late 2030s, while remaining nearly unclaimed in the US/EU.
