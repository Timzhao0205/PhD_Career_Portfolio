## 1. Landscape read

**Assignee × filing-year.** Four strata; one major correction to the first-pass read. (1) **CAS institutes own facility-scale 4Q and pulsed construction.** ASIPP filed the water-cooled anti-parallel thyristor module in 2016 (CN106100370B, lapsed 2018); HFIPS re-filed the system-level 4Q in 2022 and pushed it outbound — US grant 2026-04-28, displays active to 2044 (US12614990B2). IMP's laminated-busbar construction (CN101478224B, 2008) expired-fee 2013; its FPGA regulator (CN101581924A, 2009) was deemed withdrawn 2011. CAS Xi'an Optics' DDS+FPGA+DAC pulse regulator (CN104570862B, 2014) is **active to 2034, transferred to QXP Technologies 2022**. Anhui University holds an active fusion-tagged ultralow-inductance coaxial valve-stack construction (CN115548795B, 2022). Institutes file construction/regulator claims and frequently lapse them; live survivors are the HFIPS system patent and the QXP regulator. (2) **CORRECTION — fast-bipolar-into-coil art is NOT all aged out.** The 1999-2005 layer displays expired (Philips/Picker modular unipolar-PWM + polarity module, EP1085339B1/US6323649B1, 2020; GE coupled-inductor ripple injection, US6930483B2, ~2023). But a **live 2016-2018 MRI-scoped generation exists on CN priorities**: Shanghai Neusoft Medical holds a trio — interleaved dual H-bridge at 90° + DM/CM output filter into the coil (US10877117B2), N paralleled working bridges + N freewheeling half-bridge branches (US10718834B2), two-tap delay-compensated state-space current controller (US10962614B2) — US grants live to ~2036-38. GE holds a live shared HF-bus multi-amplifier gradient driver (US10921403B2, 2018). (3) **Generic multi-quadrant estates ring the space:** ABB Schweiz's interleaved multi-channel multi-level multi-quadrant DC-DC (US10978948B2, priority 2014-01-15, **active to 2034-12-28**) is the top live architectural blocker; classic telecom/bench 4Q (GTE US4399499A, Kaman US5428522A, Micro Control US5317254A, Forbro US3566292A, LHV US7710745B2, Technical Witts US6437999B1) displays expired/expired-class through ~2025. (4) **JP vendors file adjacent, not on-point:** TMEIC — transformer-inrush feedforward control (JP6770656B1, to 2039); Kikusui — bidirectional CC-startup housekeeping (JP7405885B2, to 2042); Hitachi synchrotron art claims main+corrective pattern summation in the controller, not converter hardware (WO2012117563A1, 2011, indicative national expiry ~2031).

**CN vendor/institute posture (zh passes).** The RMP/快控电源 tender economy (爱科赛博, 荣信汇科, 英杰电气, 通达电磁能, 新风光, 许继/华耀/科威尔) still shows **no on-point 4Q-magnet claims** under any name variant. Nuance added: 爱科赛博's one on-point filing (PSFB ZCS, CN105406724A) was rejected 2019, but it **does hold a live XJTU-acquired bidirectional family** — 3-phase PWM AFE + isolated dual-H-bridge charge/discharge, active to 2032 (CN102751772A) — battery-scoped, not bipolar-magnet. CN converter moats remain know-how- and tender-shaped.

**CN vs US center of gravity.** Live blocking families are majority CN-priority (HFIPS + Neusoft trio = 4 of 7), filed INTO the US while Western incumbents file nothing back at magnet loads; the live Western estates (ABB, GE) are generic-load or MRI-system-scoped.

**Expiry horizon (indicative only).** Architecture/control floors display expired through ~2023 (1969-2005 filings; LHV ~2025). Live wall: ABB 2014 (to 2034-12-28) → Neusoft/GE 2016-18 (~2036-38) → TMEIC/Kikusui 2019-22 (2039-42) → HFIPS 2022 (2044). Verify before reliance.
