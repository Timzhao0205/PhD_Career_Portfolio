## 2. Landscape read

**Three filing eras.** (a) **2001–2010 CN cable-craft era:** IEE-CAS with cable partners filed solder-joint methods and heated-clamp splice fixtures (CN1416991A, CN1293986C) — all display lapsed for fees; pure prior-art substrate now. (b) **2013–2017 persistent-mode era (KR/JP):** K-Joins' twin families (KR101374212B1 apparatus; WO2014157780A1 method) claimed vacuum diffusion joining of REBCO with oxygen-anneal recovery — KR displays expired-fee-related, WO ceased; RIKEN (EP3104374B1), Fujikura (EP3174072A4 family, US member granted) and Furukawa (US11289829B2) filed lap-connection structures that remain active. (c) **2017–2020 fusion wave:** Tokamak Energy's exfoliated-ReBCO bridge joint (US10840616B2, family granted in US/EP/JP/KR/CN/CA/RU) and the MIT/CFS VIPER lineage — pressed-indium demountable cable joint (JP7508555B2 family) and partitioned petal-by-petal joint (US12062879B2) — form the active blocking core, with priorities 2017–2020 and displayed expiries 2038–2041.

**Dense vs empty.** Filing is dense on joint **structures** (lap, bridge, embedment-reinforced, petal-partitioned) and **fabrication methods/fixtures** (diffusion chambers, solder clamps, simultaneous multi-lap soldering — KR101374212B1, CN1293986C, WO2013122283A1). It is **empty on measurement and verification**: across all five passes, not one family claims joint-resistance measurement integrated with the joining fixture, the production line, or an acceptance decision. The only measurement-method art found is a lab-style persistent-current/field-decay QC method (US10401393B2, US Army) and generic G01R 27/20 contact-resistance art outside superconductivity. K-Joins *describes* field-decay verification of ≤1e-15 ohm joints in its spec but does not claim it (WO2014157780A1).

**CN vs US center of gravity.** CN filings are institute/university fabrication methods (SJTU CN114464365A + CN105869782B; ASIPP CN106158139B; IEE-CAS-era craft) with a high fee-lapse rate — four of seven CN records display lapsed. One live 2025 utility model (CN223284772U) shows continuing CN activity in cable/magnet construction, 10-year term to 2035. Enforceable weight sits West/Japan: MIT/CFS, Tokamak Energy, Furukawa, Fujikura hold granted apparatus/structure claims; SJTU is the exception — its CN family reached US/EP grants (US12412680B2, EP4191615B1). ASIPP joint-specific filings did not surface (its 26.8 T-era joint art may be unpublished, trade-secret, or outside my query set — bounded).

**Expiry horizon (indicative wording only).** The oldest still-active blocking art displays expiry ~2031 (Hitachi US8385994B2) and ~2034 (RIKEN EP3104374B1); the fusion-wave core (TE 2038, Furukawa 2038, MIT/CFS 2040-2041) dominates the 2029/2030 launch window. The 2003–2013 fixture-apparatus art (CN1293986C, KR101374212B1) already displays lapsed — displays as expired; verify before reliance. Net: a 2026-2028 filer faces structure/method claims everywhere but an open field on verification/QC claims.
