## Card 1 — CAS HFIPS / ASIPP (中国科学院合肥物质科学研究院 / 等离子体物理研究所)

- **Identity:** State institute; runs EAST, CRAFT ("夸父"), BEST magnet programs. Buyer + in-house winder + IP holder.
- **Capability/price signals:** In-house NI expertise: 26.8 T all-REBCO NI magnet (2024-01, multi-width tape, double-pancake, SSTC tape) beat the 26.4 T MIT/SuNAM record [CS-B-03]. Procurement is the real channel: 关联业务 disclosures list 超导线圈绕制生产线合同, TF试验绕组绕制及端子制造, TF匝间绝缘包绕, 真空压力浸渍固化, 中场模型线圈后浸润处理及检测 — winding, wrapping, VPI and inspection let as SEPARATE contracts [CS-B-01][CS-B-02]. D型高温超导磁体系统 went to 二次招标 (re-tender) at ¥6.38M budget, 18-month delivery (IPP-2024115250113; portal cert-blocked, snippet-level) [CS-B-18]. Tritium-platform tenders alone ¥1.345B (2025-11) show BEST-wave procurement scale [CS-B-05].
- **2024-2026:** BEST assembly started 2025-05; 100 kA HTS current leads world record 2025-07; a March-2025 "BEST CS线圈绕制生产线" tender is reported (verify in Phase 2 — portal blocked).
- **Patent posture (do not fetch now):** 中国科学院合肥物质科学研究院 / Hefei Institutes of Physical Science (HFIPS), CAS; 中国科学院等离子体物理研究所 / Institute of Plasma Physics, CAS (ASIPP). Re-fetch US11581133B2 lineage in P01/P05.
- **Weakness (evidence):** fragmentation — coil process steps bought piecemeal with no inline QC line-item [CS-B-02]; re-tender signals thin qualified-supplier base [CS-B-18].

## Card 2 — 合肥聚能电物理高技术开发有限公司 (Hefei Juneng Electro Physics High-Tech)

- **Identity:** ASIPP-rooted machine shop (origins 1978 center; company 1990); ~240 staff; EAST components, ITER correction coils, BEST 55 kA HTS current leads.
- **Product & PRICE:** Built HFIPS's 超导线圈绕制生产线 — 3 non-standard winding production lines, brand "合肥聚能", total **¥16,461,400 (≈$760K/line)**, 8-month delivery, 4-year warranty, staged acceptance per line, first sub-winding trial-article acceptance milestone; supplier retains product IP (contract IPP-20220551121, signed 2023-01-05, disclosed 2024-06) [CS-B-01].
- **2024-2026:** Continues related-party manufacturing for HFIPS (current leads, conductor modules) [CS-B-02].
- **Patent posture:** 合肥聚能电物理高技术开发有限公司 / Hefei Juneng Electro Physics High-Tech Development Co., Ltd.
- **Channel:** captive/related-party (关联业务); no merchant catalog found — the de facto Chinese winding-machine builder is not on the open market.
- **Weakness:** single-customer structure; contract is build-to-spec (buyer supplies process requirements), implying process recipe stays with ASIPP, not productized.

## Card 3 — 合肥曦合超导科技有限公司 (Hefei Xihe Superconducting Technology)

- **Identity:** Founded 2022-06; incubated by 合肥综合性国家科学中心能源研究院 (安徽省能源实验室). Magnets + equipment.
- **Product:** THE exception to "kept in-house": 核聚变用铠装高温超导线圈精密绕制设备 (armored/CICC HTS coil precision winding equipment; claims first domestic 3D-forming, chain-track-drive winding) — **2024 安徽省首台(套)重大技术装备**; 18 T full-SC magnet won the 2023 award; catalog: 18 T-class research magnets, 磁控拉晶 (MCZ), magnetic separation, 7 T animal MRI; 60+ customers incl. overseas sales; ~50 patents claimed [CS-B-06].
- **2024-2026:** winding equipment recognized 2024; magnet catalog expansion; hiring in Hefei.
- **Patent posture:** 合肥曦合超导科技有限公司 / Hefei Xihe Superconducting Technology Co., Ltd.; check co-filings with 合肥综合性国家科学中心能源研究院 / Anhui Energy Laboratory.

## Card 5 — 健信超导 (宁波健信超导科技股份有限公司, Ningbo Jianxin)

- **Product:** 1.5 T zero-boil-off, 1.5 T no-liquid-helium (solid conduction; "无需液氦、失超后可自动恢复、可远程智能控制" [CS-B-08]), 3.0 T ZBO, open magnets; magnets ≈50% of MRI cost; >50% exported (GE, Fujifilm; Philips/Siemens/United Imaging per prior corpus). Revenue ¥359M→¥425M (2022→2024), 2025F ¥580-620M.

- **Weakness:** He-free "electronics-not-plumbing" quench management now the make/buy question (C11 absorbed thesis); no fusion/NI exposure; winding know-how trapped in one product family.

## Segment structure read

Four axes, four winners. **(1) Captive equipment:** HFIPS buys winding lines from its own affiliate 聚能 at ~¥5.5M/line [CS-B-01] — a hard price anchor sitting exactly at the C12 $700K ASP assumption, and a channel closed to outsiders by 关联业务 structure. **(2) Merchant equipment:** 曦合超导 is the first Chinese company selling fusion-class HTS winding equipment externally (首台套 2024) [CS-B-06] — DD_C12's "domestic toolmakers will clone an NI cell within ~2 years" forecast has already happened on the armored/CICC side; nobody yet advertises an NI/REBCO pancake cell with tension→Rc control. **(3) Integrated producers holding process as IP:** 健信 (干式绕线), 联创 (winding + 总成 services), 能量奇点 and 星环聚能 (self-built machines) — all treat the winding recipe as the moat, none sell tooling. **(4) Upstream tape:** SSTC (plus 东部超导/永鼎, 上创超导, 西部超导's new Hefei fusion-wire platform; 夸夫超导 in materials; 翌曦科技 raising 3 rounds in 2025 for fusion magnets [CS-B-16]) — QC stops at the spool. **The unserved axis:** coil-level inline QC and recipe/traceability software — every player QCs after VPI or at LN2 test via separate contracts/purchases [CS-B-02][CS-B-04]; no one in China (or the West, per CS-A/DD) sells during-winding Rc estimation, defect-map-synchronized feed, or per-coil birth certificates. For C12 strategy: China confirms as fast-follower + component base, NOT a machine market — the defensible layer against a ¥5.5M domestic line is the QC/software stack, filed before published (Rule 6).
