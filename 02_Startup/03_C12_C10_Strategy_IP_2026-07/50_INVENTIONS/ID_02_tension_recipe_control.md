# ID_02 — Co-scheduled tension + stack-pressure winding to a spatially graded turn-to-turn Rc; graded-Rc pancake article

Slot W-02 (22/25, INVENT) | U-007, U-012, U-002 | PL_P01, PL_P02 | 2026-07-04 Pass 2 | Claim sketch = drafting aid, not claims.

## 1. Title & field

**Winding apparatus (mirrored method at filing) compiling a designer-specified, spatially graded target turn-to-turn contact-resistance (Rc) distribution into co-scheduled winding-tension and interlayer normal-force (stack-pressure) trajectories, executed concurrently closed-loop; second independent: the graded-Rc pancake coil article.** Field: NI/MI/PI REBCO coil manufacturing; CPC H01F41/048, H01F41/094, B65H23/00, B65H59/38x; article side H01F6/06. Standalone companion to ID_01 (Rc sensing): ID_02 runs on feedforward lot calibration; ID_01's estimator is one optional dependent.

## 2. Problem & demand evidence

NI/MI behavior — charging delay, self-protection margin — is set by Rc; the know-how sits unproductized in magnet labs (U-001, U-012). Merchant tension control is a handling band, not a design instrument: Ridgway specs 5-40 N; KIT winds at 24 N on self-built PID (U-007 [CS-A-04][CS-A-13]). Nobody sells recipes: Startorus burned two machine generations learning; 健信 holds 干式绕线 as trade secret; no cell ships a process layer (U-002, U-012 [CS-B-07][CS-A-06]). And uniform Rc is itself a compromise — inner turns want low Rc (current-sharing protection), outer turns high Rc (charging speed). No fetched product, patent, or paper grades Rc intentionally within a pancake; WS-P01-3/WS-P02-2 and IPRT_02's re-search found spatial Rc variation only as parasitic non-uniformity, never a manufactured target.

## 3. Inventive concept

Fixed-tension→uniform-Rc is published, not claimed: Kim 2013 — coil Rc "can be predicted from the fixed tension during the winding process" [NPL-1]; Bang 2024 parameterizes Rc(B) coefficients by winding force [NPL-2]. Claimed residue: treat target Rc as a spatial field Rc*(turn, radius, sector); invert a lot-calibrated two-variable relation R̂c(tension, normal force) into co-scheduled trajectories for two actuators — payoff tension plus instrumented pressure roller — executed concurrently closed-loop, logged per turn. Two actuators are load-bearing: contact pressure at a turn is set by its own tension plus the stack wound above it, and Rc under static load drifts ~10x over load cycles [NPL-3]; a spatial profile needs both knobs scheduled against radius, with verified execution. Taper winders shape roll mechanics; this machine writes an electromagnetic design distribution into the coil.

## 5. Claim sketch (plain language drafting aid)

Lead = **apparatus** (single-actor infringement by machine vendors — Xihe/原力辰-class; visible in a shipped cell; CN utility-model dual filing). **Article independent** added as the schedule-import backstop; mirrored method at filing.

**Independent A (apparatus).** A winding apparatus for superconducting tape coils comprising a tape payoff and driven take-up; a programmable tension actuator and tension sensor; a normal-force applicator with force sensor bearing on the winding stack; and a controller that (a) stores or receives a target turn-to-turn contact-resistance distribution varying with turn index, radius, and/or azimuthal sector within one coil, plus a stored relation linking tension and normal force to contact resistance for the tape lot, (b) compiles co-scheduled tension and normal-force setpoint trajectories, and (c) executes both concurrently in closed loop on the sensors during winding, whereby the coil exhibits the graded target distribution.

**Independent B (article).** A superconducting tape pancake coil whose adjacent turns share current by direct turn-to-turn contact — free of added resistive coatings, interposed windowed strips, or bridge elements — wherein Rc varies with radius and/or azimuth per a predetermined profile (e.g., outer-zone Rc ≥3x inner-zone), embodied in the as-wound contact interface.

**Dependents (each directly off A; set survives loss of any one):**
1. Trajectories received as an externally compiled schedule file bound to a machine-readable target-Rc declaration; controller logs it and verifies execution against it (reaches import cells; §11).
2. Trajectories modulated by a tape defect/Ic map keyed to tape position.
3. Setpoints varying within a single turn — azimuthal-sector grading.
4. In-line camber/twist sensing compensating the trajectories and/or a corrective guide.
5. During-winding electrical Rc estimate trims both trajectories (ID_01 interface).
6. Per-turn executed tension/force, target, residuals emitted as a per-coil conformance record (W-05 seam).
