## 1. Closer prior art the designer missed

ID_03 §4 compares only SC art and its compliance line admits zero new fetches; PL_P04 WH-P04-2 left non-SC defect cut-out "in search listings only." Fetched now:

- **US7187995B2** (3M, priority 2003-12-31; displays Expired 2024-12-30): images a web, algorithms mark anomaly regions, decide actual defects per candidate product, select the product, and "convert the web into the selected product"; spec builds defect maps and conversion plans configuring cut lanes. This is dependent 4's "defect placement compiler" and claim-1(c)'s placement decision, in converting form — expired, so pure §103 ammunition. **Closest new reference.**
- **US11984559B2** + **US12562363B2** (LG Energy Solution, priorities 2021-08-13/-18; Active): position-indexed electrode "roll map" registered to the moving web via unwinder/rewinder encoders, corrected for length change (stretch/shear/removal), and re-registered across break/splice via reference-point comparison. Dependent 2's registration story — encoder fusion + length-error correction + re-zeroing at physical features — is live claimed art in battery webs.
- **US5531391A** (Murata, 1993; displays expired) and CN-jurisdiction analog **CN102161451B** (Murata, priority 2010-02-24; Active to 2031): winding-stage detect→cut→remove→splice→restart with frequency-tiered escalation (bulk length removal when defect rate exceeds a preset). Splice-out and tiered response during winding are 30 years old in yarn, en + zh.
- **US8714472B2** (Kimberly-Clark, 2010; Active to 2033): during-winding scan, defects classified by winding-process parameter, controller aborts/culls/transfers rolls — defect-triggered winder control actions are old.
- **NPL:** Wang et al., *Soft Science* 2:12 (2022, fetched): SuNAM practice — "wider tapes are used in areas where the vertical field of the magnet is larger and narrower tapes... in the central area" to raise margin. Field-position-aware conductor allocation is *published SC practice*. (SPARC TFMC paper arXiv:2308.12301 fetched; no sorting statement at fetched depth — probe bounded.)

No fetched reference shows a winding controller consuming an a-priori producer Ic map — the §102 gap survives. The §103 does not.

## 2. Obviousness attack

US7854057B2 (re-fetched; claims Ic characterization at positions along length, dataset distributed with tape; displays expiry 2026-12-08) hands the skilled artisan the map. 3M teaches dataset→plan→execute on web equipment; Murata teaches cut/splice at defects during winding; LGES teaches keeping such a dataset registered over stretch and splices; the Soft Science review supplies the SC motivation (allocate margin to field position). Every limb of sketched claim 1 is a known element doing its known job; the SC preamble and "conductor-quality" data label are field-of-use dressing. What survives in no fetched reference: (i) sensed signatures of mapped conductor defects used as registration fiducials; (ii) placement chosen against a stored per-turn Ic(B,θ)-margin model. Both sit in dependents 2/4 — the wrong place.

## 3. Enablement / budget realism

$20.25K is plausible hardware, but the ≤±5 cm/km registration demo carries the filing and the **weakest BOM assumption is the fiducial chain**: the $750 camera "reads printed meter marks" while no BOM line prints or applies durable marks to 4 mm REBCO/co-wind; without dense fiducials, the disclosure's own arithmetic (1.5–5.5 m/km uncorrected) is 30–100× the target. Second defect: the minimal rig's action set is "derate + flag + record," yet M5 promises winds "exercising all four actions" — splice-out and placement-shift need the splice station and planner only the full cell carries. 200 m of offcuts ≈ 2–4 pancakes: the M6 A/B result will be anecdote, not statistics.

## 4. Contamination probe

Four-prong PASS is credible: coil machinery sits outside the WBG/instrumentation SOW; BOM is COTS/home-bench; inputs are fetched public art; the planner-software embodiment is reducible on a personal laptop (strengthens prong 2). Probes: the guard-window/peak-field-turn intuition plausibly traces to Hinetics-era coil experience — the v0 distinction (lateral laser-alignment servo; no dataset, registration, defect logic, or record) is well drawn and no mechanism is reused, but the UIUC/Hinetics agreement search must close before filing. No unpublished group results needed (Ic(B,θ) surfaces are public-database). No BLOCK.

## 5. Design-around ease

Cheapest competitor path: an **offline wind-plan compiler** — software ingests the vendor map and emits start offset/splice list/feed schedule as G-code for any stock CNC winder. Claim 1 is apparatus; the winder then holds a plan, not the dataset; nobody builds the claimed machine, and §11(d) concedes no companion method/media claim exists. A tape vendor can give that software away to sell tape — exactly the 2029 wedge ID_03 exists to hold. Also, the "operator flag" limb makes claim 1 read on any inspect-and-flag winder (US8714472B2-class): invalidity bait that adds nothing.

## 6. Strategic value

Fixed, the claim binds merchant machine builders and retrofit integrators (SuperGenics/曦合-class) — the correct Phase-1 targets — and feeds ID_07/W-19. As sketched, it binds neither tape vendors (map dies at spool; software give-away) nor self-building fabs; that is a vanity apparatus filing.
