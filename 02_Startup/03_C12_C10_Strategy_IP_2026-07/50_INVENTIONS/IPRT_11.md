# IPRT_11 — Red-team review of ID_11 (terminal-only retrofit protection for NI/PI coils)

Reviewed 2026-07-04 against ID_11, W-14, U-025, PL_P09, PL_P02. All new documents fetched this day; fragments appended.

## 1. Closer prior art the designer missed

- **US11054481B2** (Battelle/INL, prio 2019-03-19, active to 2039): claim 1 = excite a device under test with a **multispectral excitation signal while under load**, sample, subtract estimated load response via out-of-band intervals, estimate impedance. That is limbs (a)-(b)-(d) of claim 1 on a powered DUT, nearly verbatim — and claim 1 recites "device under test," not "battery." Dual threat: 103 art against the detection limb AND a live inbound FTO question on our own probe stage (counsel).
- **US9851414B2** (Battelle, SOS impedance-measuring apparatus, active to 2035): the apparatus form — differential SOS current sources injecting at terminals, per-frequency impedance computation.
- **US6549017B2** (Georgia Tech, prio 2000, **expired 2021** — free to combine): on-line FRA of an **energized winding** using naturally occurring transients, transfer function compared to a **stored baseline** to call winding faults. Cited as prior art only; ID_11 correctly keeps every transformer embodiment out (Magnefy wall holds), but FRA art still reads on the method logic.
- **EP3014634B1 re-read** (CERN CLIQ, EP-only, to 2034): ID_11 §4 files CLIQ under "deeper-inside art." Wrong. The fetched claim/spec expressly enable connection "**at the beginning or end of the coil or magnet**": a trip-triggered AC-injection source at the end terminals, heating the winding to quench, then extraction. The response limb (e) is affirmatively taught at the terminals, not a bounded absence.
- **NPL:** Wang/Hahn et al., SUST 26 (2013) 035012 — τ and Rc extracted from charging/sudden-discharge terminal responses, equivalent-circuit model; Zhang et al., JSNM 30 (2017) 387-393 — terminal transient **and swept-AC frequency response** of an NI coil, frequency-dependent characteristic resistance (the claimed "impedance dispersion" measurand, published); Dong et al., APL 121 (2022) 194101 — a terminal-connected passive shunt protecting an NI magnet at 4.2 K, so terminal-attached NI protection hardware per se is published.
- Verified for the designer: **US11551840B2** (FSU sibling, to 2037) still requires the reference-field sensor in every independent claim — the [P-028] hinge holds today; continuation watch stays. ZH probe (超导磁体 失超检测 阻抗/注入/交流) surfaced nothing beyond ledger art (bounded).

## 2. Obviousness

KSR frame: apply a known monitoring technique (in-operando multitone impedance w/ baseline: US11054481B2, US9851414B2, US6549017B2) to a device whose terminal model is textbook-published (Wang 2013; Zhang 2017), then bolt on a known terminal-connectable quench-spreading discharge (EP3014634B1 end-connection; WO2020104807A1 radial heating) and a 40-year-old breaker+resistor dump. Claim 1 as sketched is an aggregation; every limb performs its known function. The genuine residue — counter-pulse **shaped from the self-acquired baseline τ** and the plural-setpoint self-characterization with rate-discriminated tripping — sits only in dependents 4-5, unclaimed structurally in claim 1.

## 3. Enablement / budget

kJ/77 K cannot carry MJ/4-20 K breadth: (i) toy-coil τ ≈ 0.3-0.8 s (Wang 2013) vs minutes-to-hours for MJ NI magnets — the probe band and detection-latency story do not transfer; μHz-region tracking drowns in supply drift. (ii) Counter-pulse authority scales as V_bound/L while stored energy scales as LI²/2; at 1 kV into henries the "volumetric dump heater" is an unsupported extrapolation. Weakest BOM line: the **$1,200 counter-pulse stage (400 A MOSFET bridge + supercaps)** as sole physical evidence for the MJ response limb — proving "spread vs plain dump" on a kJ coil needs calorimetry the $1,100 ground-truth line cannot buy amid LN2 boiling noise. Also unpriced: the baseline is really supply+leads+coil; a supply swap invalidates it (claim-construction gift).

## 4. Contamination

Prongs 1/2/4 plausible; two soft spots: (i) lock-in/synchronous-demodulation craft is the founder's Stanford day-job instrumentation (stellarator sensing, battery imaging — the latter plausibly uses injected AC); §8 never draws the ID_09-style "generic EE skill vs work product" line for the probing art. (ii) "Pre-Stanford applied-SC background" = UIUC/Hinetics NI work; if the dispersion insight leans on unpublished Hinetics coil τ/Rc data, the UIUC wall is pierced — §8 only promises paperwork checks for the winding lane. Curable by drafting; no hard block found.

## 5. Design-around

Cheapest competitor path: passive NI-aware detector (listen to ramps/ripple, customer-entered L/Rc — no probe (a), no self-baseline (c)) + plain dump (no (e)). Three of six limbs gone; it is also the version conservative MJ owners will actually buy. The drafted fallback — passive ramp-response second independent — is the **obvious version**: US6549017B2 (passive in-service transients + baseline) plus NI charging literature anticipates it. The 2029 retrofit wedge is not protected by claim 1 as sketched.

## 6. Strategic value

Does not block TE (in-build access, conceded) nor OEM embed responses; blocks only probe-injecting retrofit boxes — possibly an empty set if passive suffices. Not pure vanity: no fetched art shows detection and response coupled through one self-measured τ model at two terminals. That residue is the filing, or there is none.

## Verdict: REWORK

Required changes: (1) rebuild claim 1 around the residue — response trajectory **computed from the apparatus's own commissioning-acquired time-constant model** (merge deps 4-5 into the independent); (2) rewrite §4: recharacterize EP3014634B1 (end-terminal connection enabled), add US11054481B2/US9851414B2/US6549017B2 + Wang 2013/Zhang 2017/Dong 2022 as distinguished art; narrow the bounded-absence claim to "no NI-specific τ-parameterized terminal detection+response"; (3) demote the passive-tracking independent to dependents or drop; (4) add MJ τ/voltage-authority scaling math to the provisional content and a supply-recalibration dependent; (5) add §8 lock-in-craft wall sentence + UIUC NI-data provenance statement; (6) counsel adds: US11054481B2 FTO on the probe stage.
