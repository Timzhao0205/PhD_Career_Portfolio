# IPRT_13 — adversarial review of ID_13 (multi-channel per-coil 4Q array drive)

Red-team run 2026-07-04. All new patents fetched this run (Google Patents/FPO); NPL fetched at listed URLs. Verdict: **REWORK**.

## 1. Closer prior art the disclosure missed

§4 charted only magnet-world patents. The killing art is the customer's own paper plus multi-axis servo practice:

- **Canis paper (NPL — Thea itself; arXiv:2503.18960, submitted 2025-03-19; IEEE TAS version exists).** Nine independent Magna-Power SL10-250 supplies; a per-coil "coupling box" with "a high-accuracy current feedback sensor, and mechanical relays in an 'H-Bridge' configuration"; relays that "provide isolation between each magnet and its power supply in the event of a quench or other fault condition"; an FSCS algorithm governing "nine independent Power Supply Units" in closed loop. Per-coil channel, per-coil sensor, per-coil fault isolation, array supervisor: all 102 art four years before the planned 2029-Q1 filing — and it motivates the substitution, saying relays were chosen only because currents are low.
- **US10187001B2 (Fanuc, priority 2016-10-31; displays Expired–Fee Related):** one shared converter/DC link; per-axis torque cut-off circuits gated by a safety signal; a safety monitor circuit acting without upper-controller intervention — elements (a), (b)-class, (e) in expired servo art.
- **Beckhoff AX8000 (NPL, catalog product):** supply + axis modules auto-connected via AX-Bridge; "the DC link connection enables the exchange of energy during acceleration and braking procedures, where the regenerative braking energy is primarily stored in the common DC link"; per-axis STO/SS1 "via hard wiring and via FSoE." Dependent 2's recycling and the hardwired per-channel safety chain are shipping practice.
- **EP0454298A2 (GE, priority 1990-03-26; withdrawn; US member US5066914A):** plural gradient amplifiers, switch matrices, failed-amplifier isolation, offline spares switched in — array-amplifier supervision predating ESRF [P-143] by 25 years.
- **CN119518884A (时代星云, priority 2022-08-17, pending; granted parent CN115441487B):** shared-DC-bus storage, per-subsystem bidirectional DC/DC with independent EMS, threshold choreography making survivors hold the bus as members exit at SOC budgets — per-channel energy-budget governance, in zh.

## 2. Obviousness attack

Canis + GE US10921403B2 [P-134] (per-coil H-bridges on one shared bus) + Fanuc/AX8000 (hardwired per-axis safety; inter-axis bus energy exchange) reaches every element of claim 1. The SC residue is thin: freewheel/dump across magnets is Lake Shore/AMI-era practice (IPRT_10); the "energy budget" is ½LI² arithmetic; element (f)'s "stored coordinated response pattern" is data plus firmware — functional claiming at the point of novelty, with 112(f)/112(b) exposure. What is genuinely structural — dwell-rated freewheel, quench-trip crosslink, make-before-break bypass — sits in dependents, not claim 1.

## 3. Enablement / budget realism

Claim 1 has no current limit: full scope covers ±140-200 A channels holding seconds-class freewheel dwell at MJ budgets; the demo shows 20 A/kJ. Weakest BOM line: **$750 for backplane + busbar + make-before-break slot-bypass contacts** — MBB DC contacts hot-commutating an inductive load without arc damage are specialty parts; claim 3's star feature rides the cheapest line. Second: $700 for eight precision 4Q bridges buys hobby-grade zero-crossing behavior — the anti-relay selling point. The 140 A dwell-rated freewheel, the hard part, is never built.

## 4. Contamination probe

Prong 1 rests on "converters sit outside the PhD lane," yet §8 cites the founder's HSX GaN letter — simultaneously prior art and evidence that stellarator-coil power electronics happened inside the funded orbit (ground rules flag NSF funding on HSX work). Counsel must read its funding acknowledgment before trusting prong 1. Dependent 6 (scientific telemetry streaming) tracks the founder's diagnostics-instrumentation craft — keep generic or cut. The shared ID_08/ID_10 bench inherits their lab-instrument tripwire; choreography conception needs notebook anchors to public sources (Canis paper, trim-coil ops). UIUC posture holds.

## 5. Design-around & the Princeton elements run

**Lineage defect:** US12100520B2 full text at FPO (fetched twice this run) displays **20 claims**; claim 20 adds control coils "communicatively coupled to a controller"; no claim recites power supplies or switching systems — that language is specification-only. PL_P08/W-15/ID_13 §4/§11's "claims 28-32" did not verify (possibly a pre-grant/family claim set); counsel question 1 targets claims the granted text as fetched does not contain. Consequence: clearance is easier (no stellarator in the cabinet → claims 1-20 unmet), but the 2022 spec is 103 art for supplies-adjusting-subsets + switching, and continuations can still mint such system claims. The real swallow risk is Thea's unpublished 2025-2026 Eos power filings: a 2029-Q1 provisional races three years behind the archetype customer. Cheapest around: de-integrate — 2-3-channel bricks, sequencer in facility software, standard interlock loop; no single apparatus meets N≥4 + sequencer (divided infringement; lab-integrator culture, U-018). Second: Canis-style independent front ends kill element (a) cheaply.

## 6. Strategic value

As sketched, claim 1 blocks only integrated copycat cabinets: iTest/CAENels rack-plus-crate responses walk around it; Actionpower's CN121785095A/CN121772129A stay single-supply; Thea self-builds (suing the anchor customer is no plan). Durable value: slot-bypass freewheel continuity, dwell-rated freewheel + quench crosslink, and a cheap CN utility model on the backplane. Not vanity — mis-aimed and late.

## Verdict: REWORK

Required: (1) re-anchor §4/§11 to the verified 20-claim US12100520B2 set; correct PL_P08/W-15 upstream; handle the spec as 103 art + family watch. (2) Rebuild §4 around Canis/Fanuc/AX8000/GE; promote dwell-rated freewheel, hardware quench-trip crosslink, and MBB bypass into claim 1 with defined structure and a dwell-rating measurement protocol. (3) Add a claim on the backplane/crosslink assembly itself to catch de-integrated bricks. (4) Reprice the slot-bypass line; add one 140 A-class freewheel-dwell thermal demo or narrow scope. (5) Pull the provisional to 2027-Q4/2028-Q1 — 2029-Q1 loses the race to Thea's cadence. (6) Counsel reads the HSX GaN letter funding acknowledgment before prong 1 is asserted.
