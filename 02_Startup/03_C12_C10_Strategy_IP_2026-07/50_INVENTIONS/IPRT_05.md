## 3. Enablement / budget

Weakest BOM assumption: 80 GaAs dies ($600) + $900 mux/readout + DIY Helmholtz calibration against a $1,000 used gaussmeter yielding maps good enough to make a **zoned-Rc + joint-R + Ic-depression joint fit identifiable on one pancake**. LBNL had spatially separable conductors and still extracted only lumped per-cable parameters. Gross-defect ROC ≥0.9 at $9.6K: plausible. τ ±20% plus mode separation with a $400 DIY nV front-end beside a MOSFET crowbar: optimistic; expect binary detection, not certificate-grade parameter extraction.
