# IPRT_01 — adversarial review of ID_01 (inline Rc estimation during NI/MI winding)

Red-team run 2026-07-04. New patents fetched this run at Google Patents; NPL fetched at listed URLs. Verdict: **REWORK**.

## 1. Closer prior art the disclosure missed

- **US12196792B2** (Shanghai Jiao Tong Univ., priority 2023-05-22, displays Active) — granted method for per-measurement-unit turn-to-turn resistivity DISTRIBUTION in NI HTS coils: voltage-vs-time at multiple positions during charging/discharging, equivalent-circuit model, fit → TTR array. Post-wind, but it is claim-1 element (e) — "estimator + stored electrical model" — already granted, to a CN university adjacent to 上海超导. §4 must be rebuilt around it; counsel must read whether claim 6 / the E2 estimator practices this method during test-current charging (CN sibling unverified; only US20240302417A1 displayed).
- **US2703207A** (GE, filed 1946) — resistor winding machine measuring the wound turns' resistance per turn DURING winding via a contact riding the newest turn, Wheatstone bridge against a master, closed-loop correction. Elements (c)-(f) in 1946 hardware. Expired; 103 fuel.
- **US4416057A** (Western Electric, priority 1981) — electrical integrity testing of a coil AS IT IS WOUND; fault → alarm, stop, unwind, repair, resume. Claim 5's gate/rework logic, 45 years old.
- **NPL:** Kim/Hahn/Iwasa, IEEE TAS 2013 — tension→Rc (12 N ~727 μΩ vs 20 N ~132 μΩ) published 13 years ago; Mato/Kim/Noguchi, SUST 36 015012 (2023) — LFAC: time-varying (0.1-100 Hz) terminal test current extracting Rc = element (d) physics, post-wind; Lu et al. (NHMFL), arXiv:2604.15587 (2026-04) — Rc collapses under pressure cycling; NHMFL abandoned dry-contact Rc control for coatings.
- Bounded good news: no NPL found in these passes doing per-turn Rc logging during winding of an NI coil. Novelty survives my sweep; obviousness does not rest.

## 2. Obviousness attack (strongest combination)

POSITA knows Rc governs the product (Kim 2013), knows terminal time-varying-current Rc extraction (LFAC), knows model-fit per-unit Rc distribution (US12196792B2), knows tape-contacting injection rollers (US12050254B2 [P-025]), and knows coils have been electrically tested as wound, per turn, with stop/rework gating since 1946/1981. Claim 1 (a)-(f) reads as a predictable assembly with no element behaving unexpectedly. Un-taught by the combination: the growing-ladder recursion (one new unknown per laid turn, compaction re-estimation) and azimuth-synchronized per-turn measurement epochs. Those must move into the independent claim or first dependents, or claim 1 dies at examination.

## 3. Enablement / budget realism

$14.3K demonstrates E1 only because stop-per-turn epochs at fixed azimuth dodge the killer: the spiral-arc resistance between a rolling sense wheel and the inner terminal oscillates by up to ~33 mΩ per revolution — 4 orders above the 3-6 μV signal — and copper tempco (~130 μΩ/K/turn) swamps 5.6 μΩ deltas; no tape thermometry in BOM. "DC-reversal to ~30 kHz" is unsupported: 2182A delta mode ≤~24 Hz, ADS1262 Nyquist ~19 kHz, no lock-in. Weakest BOM assumption: $700 of Ag-plated idlers/slip rings/trailing wheel delivering Kelvin-repeatable rolling contact with thermal-EMF floor under the μV signal. The ±20% RT→77K gate also assumes calibration stability that Lu 2026 (cycling-driven Rc collapse) actively undermines — add a cycling-stability sub-experiment or soften the gate.

## 4. Contamination probe

Prong 1 "Yes-arguable" is honest, not a PASS. Sharper: the "pre-Stanford undergrad winding craft" IS the UIUC/Hinetics v0 period; Hinetics is a company, and unread employment-era assignment/confidentiality terms could reach inventions derived from its winding know-how. The sensing principle differs genuinely (galvanic vs optical), NI physics is public since 2011, so severability is plausible — but §8's PASS must become conditional on reading the Hinetics agreement BEFORE drafting. Stanford prongs 2-4 hold as written.

## 5. Design-around ease

Cheapest arounds: (i) inject via the payoff-spool spindle — literally outside (b) "between payoff and lay-on point"; (ii) block-granularity estimation (per layer / every N turns), dodging "per-turn" at ~80% of buyer value; (iii) post-wind per-turn TTRD mapping per US12196792B2 on a plain winder, marketed as "per-turn Rc map"; (iv) the admitted inductive interrogation, unclaimed in the sketch. (i)-(ii) are drafting fixes ("coupled to the tape upstream of the lay-on point"; "at least one estimate before winding completes" + per-turn dependent); (iii)-(iv) need claimed embodiments, not provisional-text reserves. As sketched, the claim does not reliably protect the 2029 wedge; fixed, it covers the galvanic in-line feature — the natural retrofit.

## 6. Strategic value

Real but portfolio-dependent: a fixed claim 1 blocks Broomfield/曦合/原力辰-class vendors from the cheapest process-layer retrofit (rollers already touch tape); CN utility-model dual-filing fits. Standalone it is one feature; with W-02/W-05 it is a fence. Not a vanity filing — if reworked.

## Verdict: REWORK
