## 1. Closer prior art the disclosure missed (all fetched 2026-07-04)

- **US4200933A** (displayed assignee ST Systron Donner; priority 1976-07-30; expired) [NEW-1]: microprocessor DMM self-calibrates gain/offset across all modes/ranges by switching internal Zener + resistor-divider references into the chain, storing correction constants in RAM, autocal scheduled at power-on, every 100th/1000th measurement, or on command. Internal reference + correction engine + scheduler was instrument-level art in 1976. ID_12 §4 charts only transducer-internal art; instrument ACAL is never charted.
- **CN101507096B** (Toyota → Denso; filed 2006-08-10; granted 2011-08-31; est. expiry ~2026-08, indicative) [NEW-2]: drive system executes current-sensor zero-point adjustment during non-energized states identified by inverter halt, gated on other inverters halted (noise), exploiting operating-profile windows (N-gear dwell). Limb (c)(ii) "commanded zero-current dwells + scheduler interleaved with load profile" exists at automotive scale and is about to be free art.
- **US10666279B1** (Texas Instruments; priority 2019-02-27; displays Active to 2039) [NEW-3]: power-supply controller with a self-calibration mode correcting offset/gain of its own current/voltage telemetry chain (external calibrated load, command-triggered). Falsifies §3's premise that self-calibration "stays inside the component" — converter-resident chain self-cal is already patented.
- **CN113555201A** (Yantai Dongfang Wisdom Electric; priority 2021-07-22; grant displayed, indicative) [NEW-4]: split-core precision CT with built-in calibration winding, internal calibration-signal generator, online error monitoring during live operation. PL_P06's ZH pass (校准 电源, 直流电流比较仪) never ran 校准绕组; first pass on that term surfaced this.
- **NPL-1** Bastos, *High Precision Current Measurement for Power Converters*, CAS 2014, CERN-2015-003, arXiv:1607.01584: the field's teaching text; reviews DCCT test/calibration including the calibration-winding method and calibration against reference units for <10 ppm converter applications — express motivation for limbs (c)(i) and dependent 3.
- **NPL-2** Danisense app note *DCCT with Calibration Windings* + FAQ: bought-in commercial heads ship with test/calibration windings (thin wire, datasheet current limit) for known-current injection in the user's installation. Limb (c)(i)'s hardware is catalog practice on the very head the BOM buys used. (Also fetched: US9310456B2, Hyundai — ignition-on offset cal + watchdog; fragment below.)

## 2. Obviousness attack

The defensible residue — load-inductance-aware volt-second calibration states (dep. 6), dissimilar-technology divergence watchdog guarding the reference (dep. 3), crate-multiplexed reference (E2, unclaimed) — sits entirely outside the independent claim. §3's observability-completeness argument is the invention; claim 1 does not claim it.

## 3. Enablement/budget realism

$17.6K buys a relative demo. Weakest BOM line: used 8.5-digit DMM *with fresh commercial cal* at $5,500 — market is ~$6-9K before an accredited cal. Referee chain bottoms at 10-20 ppm absolute (§7 admits), so claim 1(e)'s "ppm-class correction maintained" is evidenced only as relative drift-tracking against referees 10-20x coarser than the effect; "ppm-class" is also an unbounded term of degree inside a claim. The 90-day unattended garage campaign in a founder-built ±0.1 K air bath is schedule-brittle.

## 4. Contamination probe

Converter lane largely holds. Two probes: (a) the dissertation includes "magnetic instrumentation" — fluxgate current metrology sits nearer that phrase than the "generic EE skill" carve-out admits; counsel reads the SOW, not this file. (b) §8 treats a *submitted* (unpublished) HSX letter as prior art; no Hall element is used, so exposure is low, but conception evidence must be all-public. UIUC/Hinetics: clean. Magnefy: new CT art is grid-vocabulary only; keep the SC/electromagnet-load limitation.

## 5. Design-around

Cheapest: Danisense head + command-triggered cal card (TI-style mode, no load-profile scheduler), or factory-recal service — both skip element (e) and capture most fleet value. An external strapped calibrator box escapes "converter-internal" (CERN's unpatented practice). As sketched, the claim does not hold the 2029 wedge; U-033's economics live in the hybrid shunt+fluxgate cheap channel (WS-P06-3), currently a dependent.

## 6. Strategic value
