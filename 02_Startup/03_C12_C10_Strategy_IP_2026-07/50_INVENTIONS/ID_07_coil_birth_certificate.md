## 1. Title & field

Winding-cell record layer binding the consumed spool's defect/Ic map, per-turn tension/placement log, per-joint resistance inventory, and predicted turn-to-turn contact resistance (Rc)/charging constant into a serialized coil acceptance record emitted at wind completion and bound to the coil. Field: SC-coil manufacturing equipment (CPC H01F41/048, H01F41/06) — the claim residue lives inside the hosts' equipment classes; the record/traceability layer itself is defensive-publication territory after IPRT_07.

IPRT_07 changed where the protectable matter sits, not the demand.

## 3. Inventive concept (what survives review)

Three acts couple the record to the physical winding event: (i) coordinate transform — producer map entries indexed in meters-along-tape are re-indexed into as-laid coil coordinates (turn, angular position, layer) from logged payoff length and former rotation; no MES or tape vendor produces this (the vendor dataset dies at the spool [P-062]); (ii) event-time append — per-turn tension/placement entries and per-joint nΩ resistance entries are appended when the physical event occurs, so a certificate can be audited for binding time; (iii) physics prediction — a stored calibration (contents secret, version ID recorded) fills a predicted Rc band / NI charging constant. Per IPRT_07, acts (i) and (ii) are the only claim-grade residue, and only as dependents inside novelty-bearing host apparatus; the hash-chain/signature layer and the record-as-artifact are genericized (§4) and move whole into the published architecture.
