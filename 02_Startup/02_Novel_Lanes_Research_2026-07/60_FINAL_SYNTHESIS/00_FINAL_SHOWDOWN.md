## THE BEST-OF-THE-BEST TOP 5 (final answer for the 2029/2030 launch)

**1. C12 — Automated NI/MI-HTS coil winding cells + recipe software + inline QC — 83.6.**
The lead bet, now stress-tested from *outside* its own thesis basin: eight virgin lanes, 40
fresh seeds, and 15 novel candidates produced nothing within 12 points of it. Every HTS magnet
is wound; the winding recipe IS the electromagnetic design for NI coils; Tokamak Energy's 2025
Ridgway acquisition removed the West's only neutral machinery supplier exactly as second-tier
magnet programs need production lines they cannot buy from a competitor's subsidiary. The
founder has already built the core artifact once. Staged as machine + QC module (C33) + magnet
electronics (C11/C10 family). China = supply chain + intelligence, not market. Kill gate
unchanged: 3 paid commitments from non-integrated magnet builders by mid-2030.

**2. C10 — Precision magnet & scientific power converters, fast-dynamics wedge — 76.8.**
The verified wedge (fast-dynamics, telemetry-rich, weeks-not-months lead time, 5 kW–multi-kA)
into the fusion supply chain where power systems are the #1 surveyed bottleneck; the natural
electronics seller into C12's customer base and the designated absorber of C11's protection
line. Kill test unchanged: fusion buyers must show willingness-to-pay for lead time + dynamics
by end-2027.

**3. C06-pivot — Protection-intelligence modules for 800 VDC AI data centers — 74.8.**
The strongest non-magnet lane and the portfolio's diversification anchor: per-rack ground-fault
location + series-arc discrimination on ungrounded 800 VDC buses — the layer NVIDIA itself
names the open innovation area — sold through integrators. Round 2 tested five adjacent
electrification lanes (shore power, RTG, trolley, PTO, kiln) and every one died on incumbent
occupation, which *raises* confidence that this specific intelligence layer (which incumbents
verifiably do not ship) is the right non-magnet bet. Kill check unchanged: OCP/NVIDIA embed by
end-2027 → fold.

**4. V11 — Robotic magnet-harvesting cells as RaaS + SKU-recipe library — 71.6 (NEW; conditional).**
The single Round-2 survivor, and it earns the fallback-chain insertion rather than a top-3
displacement. The honest form is not an equipment vendor: it is per-motor/per-kg recovery
services + a growing device-recipe library (HDD, EV drive units, industrial motors), deployed
on cells the company owns. Why it lives: the EU ELV motor-removal mandate was adopted
2026-06-18 (applies ~2028-29) — dismantlers must remove magnet-bearing motors, creating
mandated feedstock; DoW's $1.4B ReElement/Vulcan chain and Noveon's short-loop process have
genuine intact-magnet feed specs the shred-everything routes cannot serve; and MOFCOM 公告62号
(REE tech-export ban incl. magnet-recycling tech, reactivating 2026-11-10) walls the US market
off from Chinese equipment while killing any China-co-dev variant — a policy moat and a policy
cap at once [PD]. Why it is conditional: whole-rotor HPMS ($19.6/kg AISC) and shred-first
hydromet flows (WD/Microsoft 50,000-lb shredded-HDD) may cap the intact premium below 1.5x
shred parity, collapsing the market to a handful of short-loop accounts that may self-build.
**Decisive experiment (<$5K, run 2026-H2/2027): structured feed-spec price discovery with
Noveon, Vulcan/ReElement, HyProMag USA — the $/kg premium for intact known-SKU magnets at
committed tonnage. Kill trigger: premium <1.5x shred parity at mid-2028.** Founder fit is
real and non-anchored: this is the winding-machine capability graph (precision
electromechanical automation + vision/force + recipe software), not the PhD topic.

**5. C01/C03 — Aviation-class power bricks for uncrewed/fast electric craft — 69.6.**
Retains the diversified-third-lane slot. Round 2's marine lane (G03) independently confirmed
the propulsion-adjacent whitespace is real only where certification stacks are light (USVs,
foiling craft) — the shore-side and PTO variants died on incumbent crowding, reinforcing that
the uncrewed-platform wedge is the only marine seam a small team wins. Kill gate unchanged:
USV-builder LOI by end-2028.

## Displacement verdict

**No V-candidate displaces C12 as primary.** The gap between C12 (83.6) and the best novel
candidate (V11, 71.6) is 12.0 points — larger than the mean full-pipeline discount itself. The
recommendation to build toward the C12 cluster (machine + C33 QC module + C11/C10 electronics),
with the 2026–2027 cheap experiments already specified in Round 1, is *strengthened* by Round
2's exhaustion of the remaining lanes.

**The fallback chain gains one member.** Round 1: C06 → C01/C03 → C27 → C23.
**Round 2: C06 → V11 (gated on the mid-2028 feed-spec kill trigger) → C01/C03 → C27 → C23.**
V11 enters at #2 on score (71.6 > 69.6 > 68.0) with one asterisk: its conviction is
Low-to-Moderate and its decisive experiment costs <$5K — run it in 2026-H2 regardless of which
primary track is active. V12's sensing head folds into V11 as an optional module (the exact
C33-inside-C12 pattern). V01's SiC arc-control work folds into the capability seed bank.

## The biggest surprises

1. **Whitespace now closes faster than a research round runs.** Red teams cut −13.9 (Round 1:
   −7.0); the full pipeline cut −17.4 (Round 1: −11.4). Three candidates were killed by events
   from the trailing 14 months: NVQLink (Oct 2025), Konecranes multi-brand retrofits (May 2026),
   ZPMC Prince Rupert delivery (June 2026). An idea that is merely "not yet done" in 2026 will
   be done by 2029.
2. **The novelty discipline held; novelty is not whitespace.** 14/15 candidates were genuinely
   NOVEL against 273 excluded concepts (one VARIANT caught: V03), yet 13 of the 14 died on
   *world* occupation, not corpus duplication. The exclusion ledger prevented re-derivation; it
   cannot manufacture empty markets.
3. **One physics adjudication beat every market argument.** V12's headline claim died on a
   materials-physics fact (Dy sets intrinsic coercivity, which has no external stray-field
   observable; GBD magnets hold Br constant by design — N35 vs N35EH: identical 1.17–1.22 T at
   955 vs 2388 kA/m). The cheapest kill in the mission was a fetched hysteresis datasheet.
4. **The lone survivor exists because of a law 15 days old.** The EU ELV motor-removal mandate
   (adopted 2026-06-18) manufactures V11's feedstock by regulation — the same policy-creates-
   the-market dynamic that anchors C12 (fusion supply-chain programs) and C06 (NVIDIA/OCP spec
   window). In 2026, policy documents are leading indicators; product catalogs are lagging ones.
5. **The November 2026 cliff reprices every China angle in one fortnight.** Affiliates Rule
   reimposes 2026-11-10; MOFCOM 公告55–58/61–62 (incl. the full REE tech stack with
   magnet-recycling tech) resume 2026-11-10; Ga/Ge/Sb deny-in-principle resumes 2026-11-27;
   §301 port fees resume after 2026-11-09 [PD]. For this portfolio it cuts both ways: it
   closes China-co-dev for V11/V12 while shielding the US recycling-equipment market, and it
   leaves C12's supply-chain-plus-intelligence China posture (set in Round 1) exactly right.
6. **Quantum-support electronics is a graveyard for merchants.** Both G06 candidates (V09,
   V10) were novel, high-scoring, perfectly founder-fit — and both died on the same structure:
   open-source reference designs (White Rabbit, QICK) + a giant standardizing the interface
   (CERN Collaboration, NVIDIA NVQLink) leave no merchant seam between "free" and "bundled."
