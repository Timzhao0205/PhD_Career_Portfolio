**2. Outbound (31 CFR 850 / COINS): no rule movement.** No NPRM or sector addition as of

sector-adding authority intact [PD-02][P-03 GEN3]. None of the 8 new lanes' hardware entered a

公告58号 licenses lithium-battery **manufacturing/formation-test equipment by name** (卷绕机、叠片机、

## 2. PER-LANE POSTURE NOTES (Round-1 archetype labels; CN/US/PAR ratings per GEN3 legend)

### G01 — Battery/storage manufacturing equipment (module/pack/BESS integration QC) — pattern (e)+(b), without §744.6
- **CN ✅** — strongest demand-pull of the eight: 规范条件/whitelist enforcement and per-cell coding
  duties (令第73号) push pack/module lines toward traceable QC [PD-18]; SOE energy-storage buyers
  procure under the sector-set domestic-content preference [PD-14]. New tripwire: 公告58号 makes battery
  manufacturing/formation-test equipment itself a licensed export class from 2026-11-10 — a
  China-based equipment maker faces MOFCOM licensing to serve non-China customers (China-side
  burden; not a US OISP sector today) [PD-11][PD-12].
- **US ✅** — FEOC/MACR accounting (Notice 2026-15) forces onshore, non-PFE battery/BESS supply
  chains from 2026 → new buyer class for QC/traceability equipment at US pack/BESS plants [PD-05].
  Section 301/tariff friction on Chinese equipment imports favors non-Chinese toolmakers.
- **PAR ⚠️** — feasible (no US covered-sector exposure), but from Nov-2026 the Chinese sib's
  equipment/tech exports need MOFCOM licenses [PD-11] while the US sib's customers demand
  FEOC-clean provenance [PD-05]; keep IP estates and BOMs separable from day one.

### G02 — Industrial plasma systems (waste/PFAS destruction, metallurgy torches + PSUs) — pattern (b)/(g)
- **CN ✅** — no new PRC controls on torches/PSUs found this half; rides 高端仪器/green-equipment
  攻关 demand [P-24 GEN3] and zero-carbon-park waste/energy retrofits [PD-15].
- **US ✅** — EPA retained the PFOA/PFOS 4-ppt MCLs; opt-in extension to 2031 slows but does not
  remove the destruction market; GenX-class rescission trims scope at the margin [PD-09]. No
  BIS plasma-specific export actions 2025-H2–2026-H1 identified [PD-02].
- **PAR ✅→⚠️** — cleanest dual-market lane after G03; keep pulsed-PSU designs clear of
  3A228/3A229 nuclear-pulse parameters (unchanged baseline) [P-14/P-15 GEN3].

### G03 — Ocean/marine electrification (shore power, PTO, offshore charging) — pattern (d)
- **CN ✅** — shore power is now obligation-with-penalties on both ship and port sides (Decree
  2025 No. 2, eff. 2025-05-21) [PD-16]; port/SOE buyers procure under the sector-set domestic-content preference →
  manufacture-in-China to sell at scale [PD-14].
- **US ✅** — §301 vessel fees and Chinese STS-crane duties at $0 through 2026-11-09 (whiplash
  risk at reactivation priced into port capex) [PD-08]; FY2026 E&W keeps water-power funded but
  inside a −10.4% EERE with baseload refocus — marine-energy (PTO) federal money flat, not
  growing [PD-07].
- **PAR ✅** — EAR99-class power hardware; the binding frictions are procurement-side, not
  export-control.

### G04 — Industrial process-heat electrification (>1000 °C resistive, thermal-battery interfaces, HTHP drives) — pattern (d)
- **CN ✅** — 52 national zero-carbon parks (绿电直连, incremental-grid access) are exactly the
  demand aggregators for park-level electrified heat [PD-15]; mining/coal-base green-power
  integration adds load [PD-17]; equipment sales into SOE parks take the sector-set domestic-content preference [PD-14].
- **US ⚠️→✅** — no export-control exposure, but the federal demand-side thinned: EERE −10.4%
  refocused to baseload; no new Industrial Heat Shot funding action identified this half [PD-07].
  Storage-coupled heat (48E) now carries FEOC compliance accounting [PD-05]. Economics must close
  on power prices, not grants.
- **PAR ✅** — same profile as G03.

### G05 — Photonics/laser systems (FSO terminals, power beaming, beam control) — pattern (h), dual-use heavy
- **CN ⛔** — unchanged China embargo posture for space/defense-adjacent photonics [P-19 GEN3];
  Sept-2025 entity tranche added Chinese photonics/quantum-adjacent procurement channels
  (23 China entities incl. CAS National Time Service Center for space-domain + quantum
  acquisition) — customer/supplier screening tightened, not loosened [PD-03].
- **US ✅** — the Aug-2025 USML targeted-revisions final rule (eff. 2025-09-15) moved GNSS
  anti-jam/anti-spoof items to the EAR but did **not** liberalize laser/optical communications
  terminals — FSO terminals for space remain ITAR/9x515-shadowed; plan US-only, USP-heavy team
  [PD-04]. No new laser ECCN action 2025-H2–2026-H1 identified [PD-02].
- **PAR ⛔** — as Round-1 archetype (h): a Chinese sibling is disqualifying for defense/space
  customers.

### G06 — Quantum-support electronics, room-temperature (control racks, timing/sync, decoders) — pattern (i)/(e) hybrid, export-scope watch
- **CN ⛔** — unchanged: quantum-computer development/critical-component production in China is
  OISP-prohibited for a US person [P-01 GEN3]; COINS retains quantum, and control racks sold into
  Chinese quantum programs face who-layer risk — CAS National Time Service Center listed Sept-2025
  for quantum acquisition [PD-03].
- **US ✅** — item layer stable: no new quantum ECCN rule through H1-2026 (Sept-2024 IFR + 3A904
  regime unchanged; License Exception IEC for allies) [PD-02][P-10/P-11 GEN3]; room-temp control
  electronics are still not named in any ECCN — the watch item is COINS implementing regs
  (~2027-03) sweeping "quantum-enabling" electronics [PD-02][P-03 GEN3]. Deemed-export gating for
  PRC-national hires applies the moment any tech classifies above EAR99 [P-08 GEN3].
- **PAR ⛔** — sector test fails on the China side; US-only with allied export lanes.

### G07 — Critical-materials recycling equipment (REE magnet recovery, black mass) — inverted-(a): China holds the tech chokepoint, US holds the money
- **CN ✅ (domestic) / gated outbound** — operating a China-based recycler is open (not an OISP
  sector), but 公告62号 licenses RE recycling technology *and* production-line
  assembly/commissioning/maintenance/upgrade — i.e., an equipment vendor's core export
  deliverable — from 2026-11-10 [PD-10][PD-12]. Domestic demand hardened: 令第73号 channels
  retired packs to licensed comprehensive-utilization enterprises with 2-year whitelist lockouts
  for violators (eff. 2026-04-01) [PD-18].
- **US ✅✅** — richest US-side funding of the eight lanes: $1.4B DoW/OSC magnet-chain package
  with e-waste/end-of-life-magnet recycling as named feedstock (10,000 t/yr NdFeB target)
  [PD-06]; 45X eligibility now audited through MACR — recyclers become preferred non-PFE
  feedstock suppliers [PD-05].
- **PAR ⛔→⚠️** — the tech transfer itself is the controlled article in *both* directions
  (MOFCOM 62号 outbound from CN [PD-10]; FEOC/PFE "material assistance + effective control" taint
  inbound to US credits [PD-05]); DoW-funded customers will diligence any China-linked sibling.
  Choose a side before building the process IP.

### G08 — Rail/mining/heavy electrification (trolley, RTG retrofits) — pattern (d)/(g), SOE-procurement-driven
- **CN ✅** — NEA guidance (2025-10-28) orders scale application of electric/H₂ mining trucks at
  open-pit coal mines, underground electric rubber-tyre vehicles, mine charging/battery-swap and
  光储充放 stations, 绿电直连 at mining areas [PD-17]; buyers are SOEs → sector-set domestic-content preference
  effectively requires China manufacturing [PD-14]; zero-carbon-park list includes heavy-industry
  parks [PD-15].
- **US ✅ (niche)** — no export-control exposure identified for trolley/RTG/mine retrofit power
  hardware [PD-02]; fleet-depot storage attaches FEOC accounting [PD-05]; federal demand-side
  money thin [PD-07].
- **PAR ✅→⚠️** — workable; both SOE buyers (CN) and critical-infrastructure customers (US)
  diligence ownership trees — Affiliates-Rule reactivation makes the CN customer book
  ownership-tree work mandatory again from 2026-11-10 [PD-01].
