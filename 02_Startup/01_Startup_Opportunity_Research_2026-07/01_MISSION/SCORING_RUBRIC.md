# SCORING RUBRIC

## Gates (fail any → candidate is capped at rank "reserve", cannot enter top 12)

- G1 Launchable 2029/2030 by a seed-funded team (no fab ownership, no $50M pre-revenue physics risk)
- G2 Meaningful hardware AND software content
- G3 Cleanroom dependence ≤ medium (buying dies/wafers/modules is fine)
- G4 Names a specific high-end niche beachhead with identifiable buyers
- G5 No head-on conflict with transformer condition monitoring (Magnefy sensitivity)

## Weighted criteria (score 1–5 each; weighted sum → 100)

| # | Criterion | Weight | 1 means | 5 means |
|---|-----------|--------|---------|---------|
| 1 | Founder-fit (capability graph leverage) | 14 | Any EE could do it | Uses his rare combo (power × HTS × automation × instrumentation × SW × China access) |
| 2 | Extreme-performance edge | 12 | Parity product | Credible ≥10x or unique-capability claim, defensible with physics |
| 3 | Beachhead quality | 12 | Vague market | Named buyer class, urgent pain, high willingness-to-pay, reachable by a 2-person team |
| 4 | Expansion & frontier vision | 10 | Dead-end niche | Clear adjacent markets + a decade-scale frontier story |
| 5 | Why-2029 timing | 10 | Timing irrelevant or already late | Concrete inflection (cost curve, policy, demand wave) landing 2028–2031 |
| 6 | Technical feasibility / TRL path | 10 | Research project | Sellable v1 from acceptable-TRL parts in 12–18 months |
| 7 | Competitive whitespace & moat | 8 | Crowded, giants entrenched | Underserved; moat via know-how, data, or manufacturing method |
| 8 | China+US market strategy strength | 8 | One market only, other blocked | Strong entry logic in both, sane sequencing |
| 9 | Regulatory/geopolitical robustness | 6 | Export-control landmine | Clean or manageable in both directions |
| 10 | Capital efficiency & time-to-revenue | 6 | >3 yr, >$10M to first $ | Revenue <18 months on seed money |
| 11 | Standalone-value strength | 4 | Pure add-on/diagnostic | Product itself delivers the end value |

Weighted total = Σ(score × weight) / 5. Report per-criterion scores, total, rank, and one-line
justification per criterion in the matrix. Red-team findings may adjust any score by ±1 with a
written reason.
