# PAPER_PORTFOLIO.md — simulated positions only (no real money, ever, in this file)

Treat paper trades with real-trade discipline: entry logged BEFORE "buying" at that
day's actual close, sized per IPS rules, linked to a thesis or prediction entry.
Review monthly; close with a verdict and lesson. Include a benchmark column so wins
against cash aren't mistaken for wins against the index.
