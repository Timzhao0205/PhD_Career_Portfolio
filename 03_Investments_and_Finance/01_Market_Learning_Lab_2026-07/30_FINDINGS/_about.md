# 30_FINDINGS

Machine-readable outputs. `WATCHLIST.csv` = names under observation with evidence tier.
`PAPER_PORTFOLIO.csv` = simulated positions (no real money, ever). `PREDICTIONS_LOG.csv`
= falsifiable claims with confidence; score brier = (p_true/100 - outcome)^2, outcome in {0,1},
lower is better. Rows marked EXAMPLE are format demos — replace them.
