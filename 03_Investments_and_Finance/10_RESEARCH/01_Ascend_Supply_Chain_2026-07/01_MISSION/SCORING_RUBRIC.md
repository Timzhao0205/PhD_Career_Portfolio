# SCORING_RUBRIC (fill SCORING_MATRIX.csv; 1 = worst, 5 = best)

- exposure_evidence (x3 weight): 5 = [DISCLOSED] customer-concentration table naming
  Huawei/chain with %; 3 = [REPORTED] by Tier-2 citing filings; 1 = concept-list only.
- moat_in_tier (x2): qualified-supplier barriers, share among qualified vendors,
  second-source landscape, Hubble tie (counts up), switching costs.
- margin_capture (x2): gross/net margin level AND trajectory vs tier peers; 1 for
  integrator-style ~1% margins regardless of growth.
- growth_durability (x2): backlog/guidance quality, insourcing risk (in-house HBM
  precedent), single-customer fragility (concentration cuts both ways).
- balance_sheet (x1): net cash/debt, receivables quality (watch state-customer
  receivables), dilution history.
- valuation (x2): implied growth vs evidenced growth (simple reverse-DCF or PEG vs
  tier peers); 1 = priced for the rumor scenario.
- policy_risk_inverse (x1): resilience across the four MISSION scenarios; 5 = benefits
  in most, 1 = binary on one policy outcome.
- compliance_status: pass/fail/pending + date - NOT numeric; fail excludes from
  SELECTION regardless of score.

weighted_total = 3E + 2M + 2MC + 2G + 1B + 2V + 1P (max 65). SELECTION.md takes top
3-6 with >=1 name NOT from the highest-scoring tier (diversity rule) and documents
every rejection in one paragraph each.
