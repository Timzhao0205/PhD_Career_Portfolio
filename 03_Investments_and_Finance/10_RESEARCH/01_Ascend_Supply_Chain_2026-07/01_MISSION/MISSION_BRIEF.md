# MISSION_BRIEF — Ascend Supply Chain Research (2026-07)

## Objective

Produce decision-grade research on the supplier chain behind Huawei's computing (计算)
business — Ascend AI compute (智算) and Kunpeng general compute (通算) — sufficient for
the investor to select 0–5 names for the Bucket-D satellite sleeve. Output is research
(dossiers, scores, red teams, theses with kill criteria). **Not** buy/sell instructions,
price targets presented as advice, or portfolio actions.

## Central questions

1. **Volume reality:** What is the best-evidence estimate of Ascend/Kunpeng hardware
   revenue and unit volume for 2025 and 2026, with each input tagged
   [DISCLOSED]/[REPORTED]/[ESTIMATE]/[RUMOR]? Explicitly adjudicate the circulating
   "¥70B this year / ¥200B next year" claim and the ~750k-unit 950PR target.
2. **Value capture:** Along each tier (foundry → test → packaging/substrate → memory →
   interconnect/optics → power/analog → thermal → integrators → software/ISV), where do
   gross/net margins concentrate, and why (competitive structure, Hubble ties,
   qualification barriers)? Validate or refute the seed finding that upstream
   components capture the profit while integrators earn ~1%.
3. **Per-name exposure:** For each candidate, what fraction of revenue is
   evidence-linked to the Huawei compute chain, and how durable is the position
   (second-source risk, Huawei insourcing risk — e.g., in-house HBM)?
4. **Priced or not:** For surviving names, what growth is already implied by current
   valuation (simple reverse-DCF or peer multiples), versus the evidence?
5. **Policy scenarios:** How does each name fare under (a) further US export-control
   tightening, (b) US–China de-escalation restoring Nvidia access, (c) NS-CMIC list
   expansion, (d) China stimulus for domestic compute?

## Phases (map to folders)

## Scope rules

- Include the comparative set (Hygon/Sugon, Cambricon) for context, scored separately.
- Exclude from actionable consideration: anything failing the OFAC NS-CMIC screen
  (SMIC flagged at scaffold time); unlisted entities (HiSilicon, Huakun Zhenyu, CXMT)
  are mapped for structure but not scored.
- Diversity rule: no more than 2 selected names from the same tier.
- Correlation note: the investor's career is already concentrated in this sector;
  the thesis stage must state portfolio-level correlation explicitly.

## Success criteria

A reader who knows nothing can, from 50_THESES alone, reconstruct: the evidence chain,
what would falsify each thesis, what the market already prices, and why each rejected
name was rejected. Minimum 60 Tier-1/2 sources across the project; zero conclusions
resting on [RUMOR].
