## Tier assigned + reasoning
**Tier A.** The watchlist's starting tier (B, media-screen only) is superseded: Talkweb's
OWN FY2025 annual report [S145] states in its own words that the company has become
Huawei's "全方位战略合作" (comprehensive strategic-cooperation) partner across
Kunpeng/Ascend AI/HiSilicon/cloud/large-model/OpenHarmony, names its own hardware brand
("兆瀚") as built on Kunpeng CPU + Ascend AI accelerator, and discloses that subsidiary
湘江鲲鹏 holds two distinct Huawei-issued certified-partner credentials (Ascend component
partner; Kunpeng complete-machine partner). This is corroborated by the company's own SZSE
exchange filing [S148] and by IR replies mirrored from 互动易 [S155, S156] spanning
2023–2026. This is the strongest possible substantiation category under
01_MISSION/SOURCE_STANDARDS.md (own filing / official IR reply), not an inference from a
media screen — hence tier A.

## Exposure estimate (Huawei/Ascend/Kunpeng-linked % of FY2025 revenue)

**Method 1 — product-segment share (upper-bound proxy for the whole "domestic intelligent
computing" hardware line, not Ascend-accelerator-only):**
- FY2025 total revenue = CNY 3,171,008,206.09 [S145, tier 1, fetched].
- FY2025 半年度(H1) revenue = CNY 1,306,403,506.02 [S147, tier 1, fetched]; H2 2025 revenue
  = 3,171.01M − 1,306.40M = CNY 1,864.6M (derived; consistent with quarterly figures in
  S145: Q3 772.0M + Q4 1,092.6M = 1,864.6M — arithmetic checks).
- H1 2025 "智能计算产品" (hardware) share of revenue = 21.74% [S151, tier 3, fetched] →
  H1 2025 hardware revenue ≈ 1,306.40M × 21.74% ≈ CNY 284.1M (reconciles with the
  widely-reported "2.84亿元" figure — consistency check passes).
- FY2024 full-year hardware share = 50.44% [S151, tier 3, fetched] (FY2024 total revenue
  CNY 4,107,170,805.05 per S145's prior-year column → FY2024 hardware revenue ≈ CNY 2,071.7M).
- **FY2025 full-year point figure was NOT independently fetched** (blocked by the full
  annual report PDF access constraint described above). Bounding the H2 2025 unknown
  between "stays near H1's 21.7%" and "reverts toward FY2024's 50.4%" (plausible given this
  company's history of Q4-loaded telecom/government hardware-delivery cycles — Q4 2025 was
  its largest revenue quarter at CNY 1,092.6M per S145):
  - Low case: H2 hardware ≈ 1,864.6M × 21.7% ≈ CNY 405M → FY2025 hardware ≈ (284.1+405)M
    ≈ CNY 689M ≈ **21.7%** of FY2025 revenue.
  - High case: H2 hardware ≈ 1,864.6M × 50.4% ≈ CNY 940M → FY2025 hardware ≈ (284.1+940)M
    ≈ CNY 1,224M ≈ **38.6%** of FY2025 revenue.
- **Estimate: ~20%–40% of FY2025 revenue**, method = 国产智能计算 segment ÷ total revenue,
  range (not point) because the FY2025 full-year segment table could not be fetched this
  session. This ceiling also OVERSTATES narrow "Ascend AI-accelerator" exposure to an
  unquantified degree, because the disclosed segment bundles (a) Kunpeng-CPU+Ascend-AI
  servers (core Ascend chain), (b) Kunpeng-only general servers (adjacent, not an AI
  accelerator), and (c) Kirin-9000C/9000X PCs/laptops (a mobile SoC, not part of the Ascend
  datacenter chain) — no further sub-split was disclosed in any fetched source.

**Method 2 — direct-customer floor (different measure; not additive with Method 1):**
Search aggregation (not independently fetch-confirmed this session — see Risks/notes)
repeatedly cites Huawei Investment Holding as Talkweb's #2 customer at 25.60% of FY2024
revenue (CNY 1,051.3M), alongside China Mobile as #1 at 29.43%. If accurate this sets a
partial floor for revenue billed directly to/through Huawei, but I could not verify it via

**Net: exposure_est_pct = 20–40 (%), FY2025, method = segment-revenue ratio, NOT a fetched
point figure — see arithmetic above.**

## Risks/notes for later phases (facts only, no scores/rankings)
- **Concentration, supplier side (FY2024, tier-3 fetched):** top-5 suppliers = 81.01% of
  annual procurement; Huawei Investment Holding alone = 61.29%; Beijing Huakun Zhenyu
  (华鲲振宇, a separate Huawei-strategic-partner server maker already referenced in this
  mission's watchlist rows for 000628/600839) = 12.24%; combined "Huawei-system" suppliers
  ≈ 73.53% [S150]. This means Talkweb's own AI-server COGS structure is itself dependent on
  Huawei-sourced components/licensing — relevant to margin-structure analysis in Phase 2.
- **Concentration, customer side (FY2024):** multiple secondary aggregations (not
  independently WebFetch-verified this session, so treated as color/snippet only per
  SOURCE_STANDARDS, NOT load-bearing here) report top-5 customers = 72.75% of FY2024
  revenue, with China Mobile #1 (~29.4%) and Huawei #2 (~25.6%). Flagged explicitly for
  Phase 2 fundamentals-analyst to re-confirm directly from the FY2024/FY2025 full annual
  report text (the concentration table lives only in the full-text report, not the
  abbreviated summary, and the full-text PDF could not be parsed by this session's tools).
- **Margin structure:** management narrative and tier-3 figures both describe a
  deliberate mix-shift away from lower-margin hardware integration toward higher-margin
  software/services — gross margin 13.95% (FY2024) → 17.63% (H1 2025) [S151]; hardware
  revenue share fell from 50.44% (FY2024) to 21.74% (H1 2025) over the same window. A
  hardware-integrator/OEM-style business (buying Huawei silicon, assembling/branding as
  "兆瀚") is structurally thin-margin versus the software/OpenHarmony lines.
  Core profitability caveat: FY2025 net profit ex-non-recurring items was still a LOSS of
  CNY -40,192,156.45, and Q1 2026 ex-non-recurring was also a loss of CNY -7,754,770.98
  (narrowing) [S145, S146, tier 1] — headline net-profit swings to positive were driven
  materially by non-recurring items (e.g., a CNY 66,000,000 receivables-impairment
  reversal in Q1 2026 [S146, tier 1]), not yet by sustained core operating profit.
- **Order lumpiness:** subsidiary 湘江鲲鹏 was awarded an 8.77% allocation share of a CNY
  19.058bn ("190.58亿元") China Mobile "新型智算中心" (new-type intelligent computing
  center) tender lot announced 2024-08-07 (tier 3, fetched, news.qq.com) — an estimated
  award value of ≈ CNY 1.67bn (190.58bn × 8.77%; my arithmetic, not stated directly in the
  source) spanning 2024–2025 delivery. This illustrates why the hardware segment share
  swings sharply quarter to quarter and year to year (large, lumpy telecom-operator capex
  contracts), a distinct dynamic from steady-state Ascend-ecosystem demand.
  Cross-check: Q1 2026 balance sheet shows 存货 (inventory) +67.55% to CNY 1,031.8M and
  合同负债 (contract liabilities/advance receipts) +57.81% to CNY 490.7M, both explained in
  the filing as "项目备货增加"/"预收款项增加" (project-stocking build and new advance
  payments) [S146, tier 1] — consistent with a company mid-fulfillment on large hardware
  orders, though OCF was CNY -252.1M in Q1 2026 (down 607.56% YoY) for the same reason.
- **Governance:** director 宋隽逸 (nominated by >5% shareholder 宋鹰) abstained/dissented
  on the FY2025 annual report and its summary [S145] and again on the Q1 2026 report
  [S146], each time citing inability to vouch for the report's accuracy. Hunan Securities
  Regulatory Bureau subsequently issued a rectification order and warning letters (dated
  2026-05-07) to 5 individuals, finding that 宋隽逸 did not actually perform his director
  duties and that 宋鹰 exercised them in his place, causing false disclosure of the
  board-review process in periodic reports [S153, tier 3, fetched]. Also: parent-company
  standalone accumulated deficit of CNY -1,098,487,823.45 as of FY2025 year-end means no
  cash dividend is currently legally permitted [S145].
- **Headline sensitivity:** stock has twice drawn formal SZSE abnormal-trading-fluctuation
  announcements over Huawei-linked news — 2024-10-09 (confirming the Kunpeng/Ascend
  partnership, denying other undisclosed matters) [S148] and 2025-03-06 (denying a
  CNY-90bn Huawei-acquisition rumor after the stock lost ~CNY 7.7bn of market cap on the

- **Source-quality flag:** at least one widely circulated retail-media claim (S154, tier
