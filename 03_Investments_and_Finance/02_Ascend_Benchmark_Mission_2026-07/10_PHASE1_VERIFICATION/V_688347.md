## Claim under test

**Primary (advanced-node) hypothesis:** Hua Hong participates in the Huawei/Ascend-linked
foundry buildout named by TrendForce 2026-02-25. **Secondary hypothesis:** Hua Hong supplies
mature-node power-management/driver-IC product to Ascend-adjacent systems. Both are audited
against Hua Hong's own FY2025 annual results (published 2026-03-27) and Q1 2026 quarterly
results (published 2026-05-14) — the filing vintage fixed by A3 — plus dedicated 上证e互动
searching and four independently-dated 2026 tier-3 sources.

**This audit's central finding, established before any tier ruling can be made:** the
TrendForce/Reuters reporting chain that names "Hua Hong" in connection with Huawei's Ascend
buildout is, on close reading, about **上海华力微电子有限公司 (Shanghai Huali Microelectronics
Corporation, "Huali")** — a legally separate sister company under the same ultimate parent
(上海华虹集团, Hua Hong Group) — **not** about 688347/1347.HK (Hua Hong Semiconductor Limited,
the audited entity) directly. Hua Hong Semiconductor Limited is in the process of **acquiring**
Huali (a major asset restructuring, announced 2025-08-17, still pending final CSRC registration
as of 2026-07-04 — see "Corporate structure" section below), but that deal had **not closed**
as of either the FY2025 annual report or the Q1 2026 quarterly report. This is the single most
important fact this file establishes and it governs the tier/exposure reasoning throughout:
per the same discipline as assumption A8 (never silently equate an anonymized top customer
with Huawei), this file does **not** silently equate a not-yet-consolidated sister company's
reported activity with the audited entity's own current financials.
