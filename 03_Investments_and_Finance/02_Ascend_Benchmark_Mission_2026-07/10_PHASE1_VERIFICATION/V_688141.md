## Tier ruling: **B**

A named major-house sell-side report (tier 3 per SOURCE_STANDARDS → evidence tier B) explicitly
situates 杰华特's server-power silicon ramp in the Ascend chain [S924]; the Hubble equity stake is
filing-grade but is an investment link, not a customer link; the company's own FY2025 AR text
names neither Huawei nor Ascend [S920], and no 互动易/上证e互动 company reply on the subject could
be retrieved this session (trail below). Not A (no own-filing/official-reply naming); well above
UNVERIFIED/C (multiple named, dated, non-forum sources).

## Exposure estimate: **NOT-ESTIMABLE (point); ceilings stated**

No source decomposes revenue by end-customer or end-system. Two non-Huawei-specific ceilings,
both gross over-statements of any true Ascend share: (a) anonymous #1 customer = 30.04% of FY2025
revenue [S925; identity unproven, per A8 not equated with Huawei]; (b) DC-DC product line = 53.29%
of FY2025 revenue [S920; spans PC/auto/industrial/comms end-markets, not only servers]. The
Ascend-specific slice within either cut is undisclosed → NOT-ESTIMABLE.
