## Exposure estimate
**Channel 1 — 华鲲振宇 equity/Ascend-server-manufacturing channel: 0% of consolidated revenue.**
Method: 600839's stake in 华鲲振宇 × 华鲲振宇 revenue ÷ 600839 total revenue.
- 600839's DIRECT stake = 0% (never held one; parent held it instead — section B).
- 600839's INDIRECT look-through stake ≈ 0.97% (= 48.39% of the Fund [S251, 2023-10-16] × Fund's current
  2% of 华鲲振宇 [S255;S256, 2025-08-12]) — see section C for full arithmetic and caveats.
- 华鲲振宇 is NOT consolidated into 600839's financial statements (confirmed twice, 2025-08-12 and
  2026-05-13 [S255;S256;S257;S258]); no 华鲲振宇-specific investment-income/fair-value line is disclosed
  in any 600839 filing found this session.
- Arithmetic: 0% (direct, consolidated) × [华鲲振宇 revenue, e.g. ¥12.639bn in 2024, S259] ÷ ¥108.816bn
  (600839 FY2025 total revenue, S262) = **0%** (consolidated-revenue basis).
- Illustrative-only sanity check (NOT a real accounting figure, NOT included in the 0% above, shown only
