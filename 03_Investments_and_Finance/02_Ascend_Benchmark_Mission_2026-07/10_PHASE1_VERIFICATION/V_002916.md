## Exposure estimate (Huawei/Ascend-linked % of FY2025 revenue)

**NOT-ESTIMABLE as a Huawei/Ascend-specific point figure or defensible range.** Reasoning:
- No fetched Tier ≤3 source identifies WHO Shennan's FY2023-2025 top-5 customers are — the
  company itself anonymizes them by policy [S193], and the concentration table [S191] carries
  only rank numbers, never names.
- The best available ARITHMETIC CEILING (not a Huawei-specific estimate — a bound on ANY single
  unnamed customer) is: FY2025 #1 customer = **7.46%** of revenue (CNY 1.765bn / CNY 23.65bn)
  [S191]; FY2025 top-5 combined = **18.42%** [S191]. If Huawei happens to still be the #1
  customer, its FY2025 share would be AT MOST 7.46% — but no source confirms Huawei occupies
  any of the five ranked positions today.
- Context for that ceiling: top-5 concentration has run 16.49-19.87% across FY2023-2025 [S191]
  — structurally much LOWER than the 40.46-47.35% concentration disclosed for 2014-2017 [S203,
  snippet, historic], when Huawei alone was 16.50-29.09% of sales. This is consistent with
  (but does not prove) a diversifying customer base since IPO; it means even a continued
  Huawei relationship would almost certainly be smaller in relative-revenue terms today than
  15-4.5 years ago, independent of the name-anonymization issue.
- The AI-server PCB sub-segment specifically (the part of the business that would carry any
  Ascend-accelerator exposure) was self-described as "低" (low proportion, limited revenue
  contribution) as of Q3 2023 [S194] — the most recent quantified-direction statement located.
  The 2026-02-02 互动易 reply [S192] declined to update this with a number, giving growth
  language only. Tier-4 sources claim this sub-segment reached "~30%" of revenue by 2025
  [S201] but this figure is explicitly unsourced/self-estimated per direct inspection — it
  cannot be used per the NUMBER TRUTH RULE.
- Data-center-segment ORDER scale reached a "CNY 2bn+" tier for the first time in FY2024 per
  a tier-3 report on the annual report [S195] — this is (a) an order figure, not necessarily
  same-period revenue, (b) for ALL data-center customers combined, not Huawei-specific, and
  (c) roughly 11% of FY2024's CNY 17.907bn total revenue if treated as a revenue proxy (a
  stretch) — offered only as a rough sense of scale for the whole segment, not a Huawei
