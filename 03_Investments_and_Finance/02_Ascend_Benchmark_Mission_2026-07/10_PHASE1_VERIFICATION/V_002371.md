## Claim under test (2nd-order — stated precisely)

This is NOT a claim that NAURA sells directly to Huawei or that NAURA's filings name a
Huawei/Ascend/昇腾 business line. **No direct Huawei/Ascend link is claimed anywhere in this
file.** The claim under test is structural and two-hop: "if SMIC (and other domestic
fabs) expand advanced-logic wafer capacity in order to manufacture Ascend/AI-class chips,
that capex is spent substantially on domestic semiconductor process equipment, of which
NAURA is China's largest listed platform supplier — so Ascend-driven demand flows
*indirectly* to NAURA as equipment orders, without NAURA ever being a named counterparty
to Huawei." The audit therefore has two independent parts:
(i) **is NAURA, per its own filings, actually a major domestic IC/logic/memory equipment
supplier** (segment revenue, customer concentration — customers expected to be unnamed)?
(ii) **what tier ≤3 evidence connects domestic AI-chip/SMIC capacity expansion specifically
to NAURA order/revenue growth** (as opposed to generic "semiconductor upcycle" language)?
The watchlist's own source for this thesis, [S013] (东吴证券 Huawei-supply-chain deep-dive,
2023-05-24), is already in the ledger marked `"verified":"snippet"` — i.e., the thesis
originates from an un-fetched, generic Huawei-chain sell-side note, not from anything
NAURA-specific. This file re-derives the claim from scratch against NAURA's own disclosures
plus fresh tier-3 research, rather than relying on S013.

## Exposure estimate (per instruction 5)

**Huawei/Ascend-specific revenue exposure: NOT-ESTIMABLE.** No source found this session —
tier 1 or tier 3 — decomposes NAURA's revenue by end-customer identity, let alone by
end-customer's own end-use (i.e., what fraction of any given fab's tool purchases go toward
lines making Ascend-class chips specifically, as opposed to other logic/memory/foundry
products on the same equipment). NAURA's own official replies twice decline to quantify even
the *much broader* "先进制程 (advanced process)" revenue share when asked directly [S178,
Q40], let alone a customer- or end-chip-specific share. Any single-digit or double-digit
"Ascend exposure %" figure for NAURA would be invented, not estimated — this file declines
to produce one.

**Exposed-base proxy instead (explicitly labeled: NOT Huawei-specific — this is simply the
segment through which any hypothetical Ascend/SMIC-driven demand would have to flow if it
exists at all):**
- Method: 电子工艺装备 (IC/semiconductor process equipment + vacuum/new-energy equipment,
  NAURA's own FY2025 segment label) revenue ÷ total FY2025 revenue.
- 电子工艺装备 FY2025 revenue = CNY 36.731bn (367.31亿元), +32.57% YoY [S180, S181 — two
  Sina-Finance-family articles reporting identical figures; see caveat below].
- Total FY2025 revenue = CNY 39.353bn (exact, tier-1, [S175]).
- **36.731 / 39.353 × 100 = 93.34%** — matches both sources' own stated percentage exactly.
- Cross-check: 电子元器件 (electronic components) = CNY 2.579bn (25.79亿元), 6.55% [S180,
  S181]; 36.731+2.579=39.310bn vs disclosed total 39.353bn — a CNY 43m (0.11%) residual,
  consistent with rounding/an unallocated "other" line, not a material discrepancy.
- **Sourcing caveat:** [S180] and [S181] are both 新浪财经 (Sina Finance) properties (an
  "AIGC" finance channel and a general "财经头条" channel respectively) and report
  byte-for-byte identical segment figures — almost certainly both republishing the same
  underlying wire-service earnings-summary text rather than being two editorially
  independent confirmations. I am treating this as **one well-specified data point from a
  single publisher family**, not two independent tier-3 sources, and flag this honestly
  rather than overstating confidence. [S179] (同花顺/10jqka F10, a structurally distinct
  aggregator) independently corroborates the *customer*-concentration figure (see below) but
  does not carry the segment-revenue split, so the 93.34% figure itself has not been
  cross-confirmed by a second, editorially distinct outlet this session.
- Separately, [S182] (国泰海通证券) states "集成电路设备营收同比增长超50%" (IC-equipment
  revenue +50%+ YoY) — a *narrower and faster-growing* framing than the 32.57% growth of the
  broader 电子工艺装备 segment. These are not necessarily contradictory: 电子工艺装备 bundles
  "集成电路装备" (IC equipment, the fast-growing >50% line) together with 真空新能源装备
  (vacuum/new-energy equipment, e.g. lithium-battery/hydrogen-energy tooling — a slower-
  growing, non-IC-related line per [S178]'s FY2024 framing, where "半导体装备" CNY 26.578bn
  was reported separately from other lines within the wider FY2024 total). No source this
  session gives a further IC-only vs vacuum/new-energy-only split for FY2025, so I present
  93.34% as the (slightly conservative, i.e. probably slightly-too-low) IC-and-related-
  equipment exposed-base estimate, without forcing a false-precision reconciliation.
- **A further "advanced-logic-only" sub-estimate within that 93.34% is explicitly
  NOT-ESTIMABLE**: NAURA itself declined to quantify even the advanced-vs-mature-process
  split [S178, Q40: "占比持续增加" / no number], so no honest arithmetic path exists from
  "93.34% IC-equipment exposed base" down to an "advanced-logic-specific" or
  "Ascend-specific" number.

**Customer concentration (own filing's likely-referenced underlying data, sourced via two
structurally distinct tier-3 aggregators this session — genuinely cross-confirmed):**
- Top-5 customers = 39.02% of FY2025 revenue [S179, 同花顺/10jqka F10, as-of 2025-12-31]:
  individually 12.72% / 11.25% / 5.91% / 4.94% / 4.20% (CNY 5.005bn / 4.429bn / 2.327bn /
  1.945bn / 1.654bn), all anonymized as "客户1"–"客户5" — no name disclosed.
  [S181, Sina/财经头条] independently states "前五大客户销售额占比39.03%" — matches to within
  rounding, from a distinct methodology/publisher than [S179]. **No customer is named in
  either aggregator, consistent with NAURA's own filings never naming one.**
- Top-5 suppliers = 12.30% of FY2025 procurement [S179]: CNY 1.156bn/0.824bn/0.821bn/
  0.653bn/0.610bn (individually 3.50%/2.49%/2.48%/1.98%/1.85%) — genuinely low concentration
  on the procurement side too.

**Net: exposure_est_pct = NOT-ESTIMABLE (Huawei/Ascend-specific); exposed-base (IC/
semiconductor-process-equipment segment, NOT Huawei-specific) = 93.34% of FY2025 revenue,
arithmetic and sourcing shown above.**

## Risks/notes for later phases (facts only, no scores/rankings)

- **Customer-concentration risk profile is genuinely LOW relative to other tickers in this
  mission's watchlist**: top-5 customers ≈39% (unnamed), top-5 suppliers ≈12.3% [S179,
  S181] — a materially more diversified structure than, e.g., single-customer-dominated
  names elsewhere on the watchlist. This is a *different risk shape* from a direct-supplier
  thesis: NAURA's fortunes track the whole domestic fab-capex cycle (SMIC + Hua Hong + YMTC
  + CXMT + others per [S183]'s media characterization, itself unconfirmed by NAURA), not one
  counterparty.
- **Q4 2025 margin/profit compression is a real, tier-1-confirmed pattern with a company
  explanation**: Q4 2025 revenue (CNY 12.052bn) was the year's highest quarter, yet Q4 net
  profit (CNY 0.392bn) was far below Q1–Q3 (CNY 1.58–1.92bn each) [S175, arithmetic
  independently verified — quarterly figures sum exactly to the annual total]. The company's
  own [S177] Q5 answer attributes this to "新品验证投入、产品结构及阶段性交付节奏" (new-product
  validation spend, product mix, and delivery-timing effects) in Q4 2025, with margin
  "有望稳定" (expected to stabilize) through 2026 as high-end products scale.
  R&D expense is growing faster than revenue at the same time (Q1 2026 R&D +36.64% YoY vs
  revenue +25.80% YoY [S176]; FY2025 R&D CNY 7.277bn = 18.49% of revenue [S186, S187]),
  explicitly cited by the company as the reason net profit growth lags revenue growth.
- **Contract liabilities (合同负债)**, the one balance-sheet backlog-adjacent figure directly
  disclosed this session, were CNY 4.203bn at 2026-03-31 vs CNY 4.291bn at 2025-12-31 — a
  slight sequential **decline** (-2.06%), not an increase [S176]. This is a narrower concept
  than "orders on hand" (在手订单, which captures total contracted-but-undelivered value, not
  just the prepaid/invoiced portion) — flagged so Phase 2 does not conflate the two. Large
  unverified "在手订单" figures (e.g., hundreds-of-billions-CNY claims) circulate in retail/
  forum-adjacent media on this name; none were independently confirmed this session and none
  are used here.
