# V_000628 — 高新发展 (成都高新发展股份有限公司), SZSE 000628

## Claim under test
WATCHLIST.csv (00_PRIOR_CORPUS): "Forum-claimed control of 华鲲振宇 (top Ascend server maker)" — layer
"server integrator", evidence_tier C, source S009 (a Weibo/Sina finance roll). Core question per
task: what is 高新发展's ACTUAL, CURRENT relationship to 四川华鲲振宇智能科技有限责任公司
(Huakun Zhenyu, "华鲲振宇"), reportedly a major producer of Huawei Ascend/Kunpeng ("昇腾"/"鲲鹏",
Huawei's AI-chip and server product lines) servers?

**Bottom line: the claim is DEBUNKED by the company's own filings.** 高新发展 does not, and as of
its most recent official disclosure (2026-05-22) still does not, hold any equity in 华鲲振宇. A 2023
plan to acquire 70% was terminated 2024-04-19 and has not been revived. A related party under the
same state-owned parent (高投电子集团, not the listed company) holds a majority stake instead. See
Evidence and Tier sections below.

## Tier assigned + reasoning
**Tier A — DEBUNKED.** Per task instruction, a debunked claim is tiered on the strength of the
debunking evidence, not the original claim. Two independent categories of the company's OWN primary
disclosures — (a) the 2024-04-19 termination announcement itself [S115] and (b) THREE separate,
dated official investor-relations replies spanning 2025-05-13 [S116] to 2026-05-22 [S117], the latter

was terminated and (ii) nothing has changed since. Independently, the FY2025 annual report [S118] and
H1-2025 report [S119], both fetched in full (summary form), contain zero references to 华鲲振宇, 华为,
昇腾, 鲲鹏, or any 算力/computing-power segment anywhere in business description, segment discussion,
equity-investment disclosure, or "重要事项" (material-matters) sections — consistent with non-ownership
and non-consolidation. This clears the ≥2-fetched-tier-1-source gate several times over (5 tier-1
filings fetched). The claimed "control of 华鲲振宇" does not exist at the listed-company level as of the
most recent disclosure (2026-05-22).

## Exposure estimate: 0% of FY2025 revenue (method + arithmetic)
Method requested by task: 华鲲振宇 revenue × 高新发展's stake % ÷ 高新发展 total revenue.
- 高新发展's stake in 华鲲振宇 = **0%** (terminated 2024-04-19 [S115]; reconfirmed unchanged through
  2026-05-22 [S117]).
- 华鲲振宇 is NOT consolidated into 高新发展's financial statements (no such segment/subsidiary appears
  in the FY2025 annual report [S118] or H1-2025 report [S119]).
- Arithmetic: 0% (stake) × [华鲲振宇 revenue, undisclosed to 高新发展's own filings] ÷ ¥4,980,160,344.67
  (FY2025 total revenue [S118]) = **0%**.
- Secondary check: 高新发展's own disclosed power-semiconductor subsidiary (森未科技) sells IGBT/SiC/Si
  MOSFET modules into 工控 (industrial control), 感应加热 (induction heating), 变频家电 (variable-
  frequency appliances), 光伏储能 (solar/storage), 新能源 (EVs), 商厨 (commercial kitchen) end markets
  per S118 — no data-center/server end market is disclosed, so no alternate (non-华鲲振宇) Huawei/Ascend
  revenue channel is identifiable either.
- **Forward optionality (NOT quantifiable, NOT included in the 0% above):** a related party under the
  same state parent (高投电子集团) holds a majority stake in 华鲲振宇 (55% per the tier-1 2024-04-19
  filing [S115]; a tier-4 source [S124] claims 66.76% by 2025-08, unconfirmed at tier ≤3) and market
  commentary frames 华鲲振宇 as a future injection candidate. No price, terms, timeline, or even a signed
  framework agreement is disclosed as of 2026-05-22 [S117], so this optionality is **NOT-ESTIMABLE** —
  it is a real but entirely undated/unpriced possibility, not a current exposure.
- Top-5 customer concentration (前五名客户合计销售额占比): **NOT located this session.** This disclosure
  normally sits in the full annual report's MD&A/notes, not in the report summary; the full annual
  report PDF exceeded this session's tool capability (poppler unavailable for >10-page PDFs — see
  Filings-fetched section). Documented search trail: fetched report summary [S118] (no such table),
