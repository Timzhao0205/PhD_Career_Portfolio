## Exposure estimate
**No single clean point estimate is disclosed or derivable; two structurally different
measures bound the picture, presented separately (NOT additive):**

**(1) Hardware/Ascend-Kunpeng-product exposure — range ≈19%–45% of FY2025 revenue:**
- Ceiling: 计算产品与智能电子 (computing products & smart electronics) segment = RMB15.809bn
  ÷ RMB35.090bn total = **45.05%** of FY2025 revenue [S295/S301/S303, arithmetic: 158.09 ÷
  350.90 = 0.4505]. This is a CEILING, not a clean Ascend/Kunpeng figure, because the
  segment bundles TWO distinct hardware businesses with NO FY2025 sub-split disclosed
  anywhere found: (a) 软通计算机 (ex-同方计算机) — enterprise/信创 systems including named
  Kunpeng/Ascend servers [S299, S301]; and (b) 智通国际 (ex-同方国际) — a separate,
  non-Huawei, X86 (Intel/AMD)-based consumer gaming-laptop ODM/brand business ("机械革命")
  [S299, S306]. Treating the whole segment as "Ascend/Kunpeng exposure" would silently
  conflate these two.
