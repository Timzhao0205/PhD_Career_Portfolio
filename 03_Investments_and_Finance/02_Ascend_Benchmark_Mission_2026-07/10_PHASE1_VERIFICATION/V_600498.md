## Exposure estimate: NOT-ESTIMABLE as a precise % of FY2025 revenue (method shown)
**Method requested by task**: identify Ascend-linked (Huawei 昇腾-specific) revenue as a share of FY2025 total
consolidated revenue (¥249.19亿 [S238, S239, S240, S248]).
**Key accounting point** (unlike the 0%-stake 000628 case): because 烽火通信 holds >50% and controls 长江计算
(70.71218% [S235, S236, S241]), 长江计算 is a **fully consolidated subsidiary** — under both PRC ASBE-33 and
IFRS-10-equivalent consolidation rules, 100% of 长江计算's own revenue already flows into 烽火通信's
consolidated top line; the 70.71218% stake affects only the non-controlling-interest split of NET INCOME, not
the amount of revenue consolidated. So the calculation is NOT "stake % × 长江计算 revenue"; it would instead be
"长江计算's own reported revenue (100%, already in the FY2025 ¥249.19亿 total) ÷ ¥249.19亂" **if** 长江计算's
own revenue were disclosed — but it is not.
**Why NOT-ESTIMABLE:**
1. 烽火通信's own FY2025 disclosures (via mirrors, since primary PDFs would not parse) break revenue out ONLY
   by broad product category (通信系统设备 / 光纤及线缆 / 数据网络产品), never by subsidiary. Three
   independent fetches explicitly confirmed 长江计算 is **not named anywhere** in the FY2025 summary content
   these fetches could access [S239, S240, S248 all separately note its absence].
2. 长江计算's OWN product mix (per its own website and the government-portal source [S247]) spans Kunpeng-CPU
   general-purpose servers, Ascend-AI-accelerator servers, non-Huawei x86 servers, storage, and terminal/PC
   products — even a clean "长江计算 = ¥X revenue" figure would still require a further internal split to
   isolate Ascend-specific exposure from Kunpeng-only/x86 exposure, and no source provides that split.
3. **Unreconciled scale mismatch (flagged, not resolved, this session)**: the 数据网络产品 segment
   (¥14.20亿, ≈5.70% of FY2025 revenue) is the ONLY disclosed line that could plausibly house server-hardware
   revenue, but the government-portal source [S247] independently describes 长江计算's OWN scale as having
   grown roughly 30-fold over 3 years (exact base-year absolute yuan figure not given in the fetched excerpt)
   and separately aspiring to "突破百亿元" (break ¥10 billion) — figures of that order would exceed the entire
   ¥14.20亿 数据网络产品 line by itself, suggesting 长江计算's revenue is likely booked substantially inside
   the much larger 通信系统设备 segment (¥186.55亿) instead, or that portal-sourced "产值" (gross output
   value) uses a different, non-GAAP-consolidated methodology than 烽火通信's audited segment revenue. Neither
   possibility can be confirmed from sources fetched this session.
4. Given (1)–(3), quoting a single %, or even the naive 5.70% 数据网络产品 floor, as "the" Ascend exposure
   would misrepresent the state of disclosure. **NOT-ESTIMABLE is the honest answer.** If forced to cite one
   illustrative, heavily-caveated reference point: 数据网络产品 = ¥14.20亿 / ¥249.19亿 = **5.70% of FY2025
   revenue** is a loose, probably-understated floor (assumes, almost certainly wrongly, that 100% of that one
   segment = 100% of 长江计算 = 100% Ascend-specific) — presented as color only, not as a verified exposure
   estimate.
