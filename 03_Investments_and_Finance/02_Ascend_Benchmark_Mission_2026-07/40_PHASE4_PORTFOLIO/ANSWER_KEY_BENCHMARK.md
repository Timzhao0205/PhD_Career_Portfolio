## 1. Headline

  a value-free layer is not an investment case.

  configuration the exercise exists to teach avoidance of.

## 5. Top-3 uncertainties (where this key is most likely wrong)

1. **The Ascend-specific split is unobservable everywhere.** No company in the universe
   discloses Ascend-brand revenue. W2 was scored on the rubric's literal "Huawei-linked"
   text (R0.1); a stricter Ascend-only reading would cut 688629's and 600839's W2 hard
   and reorder ranks 1-3. If Huawei Connect 2026 (≈Sept) or H1-2026 reports produce the
   first company-confirmed Ascend order books, this key's top half reshuffles.
2. **Valuation regime risk dominates selection risk.** 13/14 names are priced for
   perfection; the key's cash-heavy answer is a claim about PRICE, not about the chain's
   physical growth (which the evidence supports). If the domestic AI-capex boom sustains
   multi-year, the 56% cash position underperforms almost any allocation — the key would
   then be "right on process, wrong on outcome." Symmetrically, in a de-rating, every
   invested name including rank-1 falls hard.
3. **Process residuals.** (a) 600839, 000034 and 688981 were never red-teamed (they rose
   after rescoring; R0.5) — their cells faced less adversarial pressure than ranks
   attacked directly. (b) Five names' primary filings never parsed (documented trails;
   mirrors used) — a full-PDF re-read could shift W1 substance either way, most plausibly
   for 301236/600498. (c) Quotes are one day's close (2026-07-07); W4 flags are
   regime-dependent and go stale fastest of all cells.
