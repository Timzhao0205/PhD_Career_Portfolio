## 3. Implied expectations

At CNY 68.21 (2026-07-07 close), TFME's ttm P/E of 71.55× and FY2025-basis P/E of 84.94× sit far above
what its 3-year net-margin trajectory alone would justify: net margin attributable to parent was only
0.76% / 2.84% / 4.36% in FY2023 / FY2024 / FY2025 [F_002156.md, S430/S427/S424, cross-checked S131], so
the eye-catching +79.86% FY2025 and +224.55% Q1-2026 net-profit growth rates [V_002156.md §L, S135
reused] are substantially a low-base margin-recovery effect (net margin roughly sextupling off a
near-breakeven FY2023) rather than steady-state economics — a naive PEG of 0.3–0.9× on those growth
rates understates how much further, multi-year margin expansion and revenue acceleration the current
multiple actually requires to normalize. The verified growth engine behind that trajectory is
concentrated and AMD-anchored: TFME states it is AMD's "primary packaging/test supplier, over 80% of
[AMD's] relevant products" [V_002156.md §H, S133]; its single largest (unnamed, but AMD-attributed by
every source that offers an attribution) customer is 52.29% of FY2025 revenue, and top-5 concentration
is 69.54% [V_002156.md §A, S131]; advanced packaging (flip-chip etc.) is ≈70% of FY2025 revenue
[V_002156.md §I, S133]. Citi's 2026-06-21 re-rating (reported by Sina Finance 2026-06-25) raised its
TFME target from CNY 48 to CNY 80 explicitly on ">50% of Tongfu's revenue from AMD" plus the AI/HPC
advanced-packaging supercycle, with zero Huawei/Ascend content in the bank's own stated thesis
[V_002156.md §M, S138] — today's CNY 68.21 sits above 群益证券's older, AMD/Meta-only CNY 65 target
(2026-02-25) [S139] but still below Citi's newer ceiling, so the verified AMD-only thesis alone leaves
room to run without any Huawei/Ascend contribution required. However, none of that arithmetic needs
the unverified tier-C "AMD+华为双绑定/昇腾服务器芯片封测主力" self-media narrative that has circulated
loudly on roughly the same timeline [V_002156.md §N] — the company's own IR record gives only a generic
non-denial to direct Huawei questions asked twice, contrasted with an explicit "no" on Intel in the
same briefing [V_002156.md §E–G] — so that narrative is best read as unpriced optionality layered on
an already-demanding, AMD-cycle-dependent multiple, not as this valuation's load-bearing assumption.

**Flag: PRICED-FOR-PERFECTION** — trailing multiples of 72×/85× P/E and 3.5×/3.7× P/S, against a net
margin that only just reached 4.36% and 69.54%/52.29% top-5/single-customer concentration in one
AMD-cycle relationship, leave little margin of safety for AMD-capex deceleration or for the unverified
Ascend narrative to be discounted to zero without material multiple compression.
