## Implied expectations

At CNY412.10/share and CNY280.71bn total market cap (2026-07-07 post-close [S642;S643]), Shennan
Circuits trades at ~77.23x TTM P/E (TTM attributable net profit CNY3,634.74M) and ~11.03x TTM P/S
(TTM revenue CNY25,459.98M) [S475;S190;S198;S644] — multiples that go well beyond what a
PCB/substrate manufacturer's already-strong, VERIFIED FY2025 print would typically command on its
own (revenue +32.05%, net profit +74.47%, net margin expanding 10.34%→10.48%→13.85% over
FY2023-25 [S475;S476;S477;S190]). For today's price to be justified, the market must be
underwriting substantially MORE than a one-year growth spike: a multi-year continuation of this
growth-and-margin trajectory, AND a meaningful mix-shift toward AI-server/HPC-linked PCB and
packaging-substrate business specifically — the implicit narrative (per Phase 1's audit) being
that Shennan is a core supplier into Huawei's Ascend AI-chip ecosystem. That narrative is not
substantiated at Tier A/B by anything Phase 1 located: verified customer concentration is
diversified (top-5 = 18.42% of FY2025 revenue, #1 customer just 7.46% [S191]) — structurally too
small and too spread a base for any single AI/Ascend counterparty to be doing the work this
multiple requires unless it already IS the unnamed #1 customer, which no source confirms; the
company's own most recent QUANTIFIED statement on AI-server PCB was "占比较低" (a low proportion,
limited revenue contribution), dated Q3 2023 [S194], with no updated percentage located through
Q1 2026 despite the company being asked directly for one in a 2026-02-02 互动易 reply and
declining to give a number [S192]; and the company has, as recently as 2026-06-03 and 2026-01-09,
declined to confirm or deny specific AI-chain counterparty relationships (Nvidia Rubin, AMD)
citing commercial confidentiality [S192]. The only sources that assert a large, Ascend-specific
revenue share (e.g., "~30% AI-related business," "core supplier to Huawei Ascend") are Tier-4
self-media claims explicitly confirmed, on direct inspection, to be uncited author estimates
[S201] — not usable per the NUMBER TRUTH RULE. In short, today's price appears to assume a
durable AI/Ascend-driven re-rating of the business mix that the verified evidence base neither
confirms nor quantifies, while the company's own disclosure pattern (diversified customers,
declined AI-revenue-share disclosure, stale 2023 "low" self-description) is the opposite of what
would substantiate it. Flag: **PRICED-FOR-PERFECTION** — a ~77x trailing-earnings, ~11x
trailing-sales multiple on a capital-intensive PCB manufacturer already discounts a multi-year
Ascend/AI-driven mix-shift that remains, per this mission's own Phase-1 audit, Tier-C/forum-sourced
and unconfirmed by the company's own (diversified, non-disclosing) customer data.
