## Implied expectations

At CNY118.08/share and CNY44.20bn total market cap (2026-07-07, ~16:14 CST post-close
[S656;S657]), Shenling Environment trades at 231.45x TTM P/E (TTM attributable net profit
CNY190.95M) and 10.53x TTM P/S (TTM revenue CNY4,198.36M) [S220;S496;S224;S658] — multiples an
order of magnitude beyond what the underlying, VERIFIED financial record supports on its own:
FY2025 net profit did grow 87.59% YoY on 39.55% revenue growth [S220;S496], but the trailing
base has already inflected DOWNWARD — TTM net profit (CNY190.95M) sits below the FY2025
full-year figure precisely because Q1 2026 revenue fell 1.80% and net profit fell 47.71% YoY,
with no customer- or product-line attribution offered in the company's own quarterly disclosure
[S224]. For today's price to be justified, the market must be underwriting a swift return to
FY2025-pace (or faster) growth driven specifically by AI-data-center liquid-cooling penetration
and, within that, a durable Huawei/Ascend-linked customer relationship — yet the diversified
segment mix means 44.34% of FY2025 revenue (100% − 55.66%) sits in industrial/specialty/
public-construction segments with no AI linkage at all, and even the 55.66%-of-revenue
data-services segment [S223] is only the OUTER bound for "any AI-data-center-adjacent business"
(it also serves telecom base stations and non-AI cloud/colocation customers per historic
disclosure), not a Huawei-specific or even liquid-cooling-specific figure; the single largest
unnamed customer is capped at just 14.30% of FY2025 revenue [S221]. Phase 1's dedicated audit
found genuine, well-documented Huawei-GENERIC exposure historically (an explicit, prospectus-era
20.94%-36.15% Huawei revenue share, 2017-2019) and a plausible-but-inferential current "H公司"
customer reference in the FY2025 filing — but the specific Huawei-ASCEND/AI-chip liquid-cooling
link the market's re-rating narrative requires is substantiated only by two Tier-C forum posts
asserting an uncited ">50% share" and "sole Ascend-384 supplier" claim [S229;S230], while the
company's own 2025 investor-relations replies repeatedly decline to confirm or deny Huawei/
Ascend/Nvidia-specific relationships by name, citing confidentiality, even as recently as
2025-09-10 [S222]. A genuine, Huawei-agnostic liquid-cooling market-share leadership position
(CCID-attributed #1 ranking in two 2024 sub-categories [S231;S232]) supports Shenling being a
credible operator in the space, but does not itself confirm the Ascend-specific exposure the
current multiple appears to assume. Flag: **PRICED-FOR-PERFECTION** — a ~231x trailing-earnings,
~11x trailing-sales multiple on a company whose trailing profit base is already contracting
prices in near-flawless, accelerating execution of an AI/Ascend-driven growth story that remains,
per this mission's own Phase-1 audit, Tier-C/forum-sourced and unconfirmed by the company's own
(diversified, non-disclosing) customer data.
