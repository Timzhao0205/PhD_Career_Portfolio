## Implied expectations

At CNY 806.39/share and CNY 585.19bn total market cap (2026-07-07, gtimg timestamp 16:14:09 CST /
eastmoney timestamp 15:34:09 CST, both post-15:00-close [S635;S636]), NAURA trades at ~104.95x TTM P/E
(TTM attributable net profit CNY 5,576.02M) and ~14.11x TTM P/S (TTM revenue CNY 41,469.95M)
[S175;S176;S460;S637] — multiples set against a FY2025 print in which revenue grew +30.85% YoY
(company's own restated-basis figure) while net profit attributable was roughly flat, actually DOWN
−1.77% YoY to CNY 5,521.99M [S175, F_002371.md] — i.e. FY2025 delivered growth WITH margin
compression, not operating leverage: gross margin fell 42.85%→40.10% and net margin fell 18.84%→14.03%
YoY [F_002371.md], and Q4 2025 alone — the year's highest-revenue quarter (CNY 12.05bn) — produced by
far its lowest quarterly profit (CNY 0.39bn) [V_002371.md, S175]. For today's multiple to be validated
going forward, the market must be underwriting at least two things neither the FY2025 print nor this
session's own arithmetic yet demonstrates: (1) a real 2026 margin recovery — the company attributes
Q4 2025's compression to transient "新品验证投入、产品结构及阶段性交付节奏" (new-product validation
spend, product mix, delivery-timing effects), with margin "有望稳定" (expected to stabilize) [S177],
even as R&D expense keeps growing faster than revenue (Q1 2026 R&D +36.64% YoY vs revenue +25.80% YoY
[F_002371.md]) — turning the company's own official 2026-2027 guidance that domestic
semiconductor-equipment capex "整体资本开支仍会维持高位，整体处在景气扩张区间" (will stay high, in an
expansion phase), driven by "AI算力芯片" (AI compute-chip), HBM/advanced-memory, and advanced-logic
demand plus accelerating domestic-equipment substitution [S177], into margin-accretive rather than
cost-diluted growth; and (2) several more years of fast growth at that pace — even taking already
Phase-1-fetched, NOT independently re-verified this session, sell-side/Wind-consensus color of
2026E/2027E/2028E net profit ≈CNY8.0bn/11.0bn/14.0bn (implied +44%/+37%/+28% YoY) [S186, cited only as
prior color per task instruction], TODAY's market cap implies forward P/E of only ~73.1x/~53.2x/~41.8x
on those three years respectively (585,188.51/8,000=73.15; /11,000=53.20; /14,000=41.80) — still rich
after three straight years of profit growth that fast, an outcome FY2025 itself (profit DOWN, not up,
despite +30.85% revenue) has not yet delivered; and for reference, the same ~81x figure S186 reported
as NAURA's "current PE" just seven weeks earlier (2026-05-20) implies the multiple has re-rated UP,
not down, since then, even though the FY2025 print it was based on (filed 2026-04-18) has not changed
in the interim. It is also worth stating plainly what this multiple does NOT price directly: NAURA is

assigns the causal link between Ascend/SMIC-driven demand and NAURA order growth Tier B (named

less certain channel than a 100x-plus multiple implicitly assumes.
