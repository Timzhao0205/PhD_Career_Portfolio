## Implied expectations

At CNY28.80/share and CNY36.29bn total market cap (2026-07-07 post-close [S621;S622]), Talkweb
trades at ~606x TTM P/E (TTM attributable net profit CNY59.85M) and ~11.55x TTM P/S (TTM revenue
CNY3,141.57M) [S145;S146] — multiples that assume much more than FY2025's actual record delivered:
revenue fell -22.79% YoY in FY2025 [S145] (after +30.21% in FY2024), gross margin is still thin at
13.95% FY2024 → 16.33% FY2025 [S145;S444;S445], and — critically — TTM net profit EXCLUDING
non-recurring items is itself still a LOSS of about CNY-23.31M [S145;S146], meaning the entire
positive headline P/E above is an artifact of one-off gains (e.g., a CNY66.00M
receivables-impairment reversal booked in Q1 2026 [S146]), not demonstrated recurring profit. For
today's price to be justified going forward, the market must be underwriting: (1) the Huawei
Kunpeng/Ascend "国产智能计算" hardware line — an estimated 20-40% of FY2025 revenue [V_002261.md,
derived from S145;S147;S151] but historically thin-margin and running through a supplier base
~73.5% Huawei-system-concentrated as of FY2024 (top-5 suppliers 81.01% of procurement, Huawei
Investment Holding alone 61.29%) [S150;S437 per F_002261.md] — converting from a lumpy,
telecom-capex-driven integrator business into a durable, higher-margin franchise; (2) the
software/OpenHarmony mix-shift (hardware's share of revenue fell from 50.44% FY2024 to 21.74% H1
2025 [S151]) translating into sustained CORE profit growth (not one-off items) fast enough to work
an effective >600x trailing multiple down to a normal range within a few years; and (3) no repeat
of the governance overhang opened by the 2026-05-08 湖南证监局 warning letters for false
disclosure of the board-review process [S153], nor of the kind of headline-driven volatility
already seen around the 2025-03 Huawei-acquisition-rumor denial [S149]. Tier-3 retail/sell-side
commentary (structural long bias per SOURCE_STANDARDS; treated as color only here, not
load-bearing, given a demonstrated internal inconsistency) puts 2026 forecasts at CNY350-450M net
profit (+367%-500% YoY) and >CNY8bn revenue (+50%+ YoY — inconsistent with each other, since +50%
off the actual CNY3,171M FY2025 base [S145] gives only ~CNY4.76bn, not >CNY8bn) with average
institutional target prices near CNY38.60-50 [S623;S624] — an echo of the same unreliable-hype
pattern V_002261.md already flagged on this name (S154's contradicted CNY5bn Ascend-revenue
claim). That such an aggressive, internally-inconsistent forecast is what is needed to make

has already fallen ~26% from CNY39.05 on 2026-03-06 [S624] to today's CNY28.80 — is itself
informative about how much is still priced in. Flag: **PRICED-FOR-PERFECTION** — a ~606x TTM P/E
