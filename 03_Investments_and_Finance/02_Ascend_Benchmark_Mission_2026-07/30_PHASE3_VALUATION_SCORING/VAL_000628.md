## Implied expectations

At CNY 48.18/share (CNY 16.97bn market cap), 000628 prices to ~155–157x trailing/FY2025 earnings and
~3.4–3.5x trailing/FY2025 sales on the F_000628 figures — multiples a shrinking, thin-margin
construction company could not otherwise support. FY2025 attributable net profit of ¥109.24M is only
0.64% of market cap and has swung from ¥365.94M (FY2023) to ¥61.37M (FY2024) to ¥109.24M (FY2025), so
the P/E is a noisy, near-meaningless signal in isolation; the steadier P/S read (3.4–3.5x) is still
rich given F_000628's fundamentals: revenue down −11.0%/−30.1%/−22.8% YoY over FY2024/FY2025/Q1-2026,
gross margin only 7.9–12.0% across FY2023–25 (construction is ~93% of the revenue mix), R&D intensity
falling to 0.49% of revenue in FY2025, and the one chip-adjacent segment (power semiconductor, ~1.2%
of FY2025 revenue) both loss-making at the segment level (gross margin −40% to −52%) and being wound
down at the listed entity — its pilot-production line was transferred to the parent, 高投集团, in
2025, not scaled. None of that, on its own, is a mid-100s-P/E or mid-3x-sales business. What the price
appears to assume instead is the "算力" (computing-power) narrative around 华鲲振宇, the Huawei-
Ascend-server maker in which 高新发展 holds a verified **0% equity stake** as of its own most recent
investor-relations disclosure, 2026-05-22 — six weeks before this valuation [V_000628]. The
2023-proposed 70% acquisition was terminated 2024-04-19 and has never been revived; the majority stake
instead sits with a sister company under the same state parent, and V_000628 explicitly found the
forward optionality of any future injection **NOT-ESTIMABLE** (no price, terms, timeline, or signed
agreement disclosed). Yet the market visibly still trades the story: a 2026-02-12 limit-up was tagged
by a market recap as driven by "华鲲振宇并购预期+功率半导体+成都国资" [S610], and an August-2025
limit-up to ¥56.89/share followed a Huakun Zhenyu capital-raise listing news item even though that
same article confirmed 高新发展 held zero equity stake at the time [S611]. Today's multiple reads less
like a valuation of the filed construction-and-power-semiconductor business than a standing premium on
an asset-injection scenario that remains filing-unconfirmed and undated.

**Flag: PRICED-FOR-PERFECTION** — a ~155–157x trailing P/E and ~3.4–3.5x trailing P/S on a shrinking,
thin-margin construction-and-power-semiconductor business require the market to be underwriting a
filing-unconfirmed, NOT-ESTIMABLE future 华鲲振宇/Ascend asset injection, not the disclosed
fundamentals.
