## Implied expectations

At CNY199.40/share and CNY93.37bn total market cap (2026-07-07 post-close [S684;S685]), 华丰科技
trades at ~216.20x TTM P/E (TTM attributable net profit CNY431.87M, built as FY2025 CNY358.57M
[S266;S549] − Q1-2025 CNY31.81M [S686] + Q1-2026 CNY105.11M [S270;S687]) and ~33.89x TTM P/S (TTM
revenue CNY2,755.43M, same build from FY2025 CNY2,527.73M [S265;S266;S549], Q1-2025 CNY405.53M
[S270], Q1-2026 CNY633.22M [S270]) — extreme multiples even set against a genuinely strong,
filing-verified turnaround: FY2025 revenue +131.50% YoY, net margin swinging from a FY2024 loss
(-1.63%) to +14.19%, gross margin recovering to 31.44%, and Q1 2026 continuing the trend (revenue
+56.15%, net profit +230.43%) [S265;S266;S270]. For today's price to be justified, the market must
be underwriting a multi-year continuation of this hypergrowth cadence AND a durable
content-per-system premium as high-speed/supernode interconnect architectures scale — a trend with
real, own-filing support (32 new production lines aimed at the AI-server market, cable-module
capacity expanding 6,000→30,000 sets/month, H1-2025 capacity utilization already 111.89% of
nameplate, in-hand cable-module orders CNY616mm as of Oct 2025 [S277]) — while simultaneously
assuming that the company's already-extreme and still-RISING single-customer concentration (top-1
customer, corroborated-Huawei, 60.52% of FY2025 revenue, up from 37.11% in FY2024 and 25.36% in
FY2023 [S266;S268;S276]) will not repeat the one precedent already sitting in the company's own
filing history — a 2020-2021 Huawei-driven whipsaw in which communications-connector sales volume
plunged 42.24% YoY and capacity utilization collapsed from 84.04% to 51.61% [S272] — nor intersect
with the defense segment's own company-acknowledged order-timing uncertainty [V_688629.md]. The
multiple also appears to price in more than the company's own, repeatedly-stated self-description —
"one of the core suppliers" (核心供应商之一) of Huawei's high-speed backplane connectors, a standing
position restated identically across at least two years [S267;S269] but on its face inconsistent
with an exclusive/dominant framing — since the specific "dominant share of Ascend backplane-connector
supply" claim this mission set out to test remains UNVERIFIED beyond Tier C: no filing or named
sell-side source quantifies it, one specific quantified rumor (400,000 sets / ~CNY6bn of
Ascend-specific 2025 revenue) is explicitly labeled "Market Rumor (Unverified)" by the one source
that addresses it directly [S278], and a current (2026-06-24) named sell-side note on this exact
ticker discusses generic supernode/AI-server tailwinds and a new CPU-SOCKET growth line without
repeating any Huawei/Ascend/backplane-connector-specific figure at all [S279]. Additional context the
multiple must also look past: capex rose every year (CNY118.07M→254.55M→395.70M), operating cash
flow was negative in both FY2024 and FY2025 despite the FY2025 profit swing, and FY2025 was the first
of three fiscal years the company shows net DEBT rather than net cash (CNY-108.32M), even as Q1 2026
swung back to net cash (+CNY142.21M) on an unusually large single-quarter operating cash flow
(+CNY427.80M) whose driver this mission's extraction does not decompose [F_688629.md]. Flag:
**PRICED-FOR-PERFECTION** — a ~216x trailing-earnings, ~34x trailing-sales multiple on a connector
manufacturer with 60%+ and rising single-customer concentration prices in years of uninterrupted
hypergrowth plus an unverified competitive-dominance premium, layered on a customer relationship
whose own filing history already contains one precedent of sharp Huawei-driven cyclicality.
