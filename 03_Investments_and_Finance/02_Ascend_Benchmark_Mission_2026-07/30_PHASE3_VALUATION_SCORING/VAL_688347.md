## Implied expectations

At CNY 324.00/share and CNY 563.02bn total market cap (2026-07-07, gtimg timestamp 16:14:50 CST / eastmoney timestamp 16:11:50 CST, both post-15:00-close [S1100;S1101]), Hua Hong's A-share line trades at ~1141.08x TTM P/E (TTM attributable net profit CNY493.41M, built as FY2025 CNY376.61M [S900;S1000] − Q1-2025 CNY22.76M [S1103;S1104] + Q1-2026 CNY139.56M [S904;S1016]) and ~31.27x TTM P/S (TTM revenue CNY18,004.28M) — multiples that, in absolute terms, sit above every other dual-listed STAR-market foundry this mission has valued, including SMIC's own ~247x TTM P/E [VAL_688981.md] and connector-maker 688629's ~216x [VAL_688629.md], against a FY2023-25 record that is real but modest by comparison: revenue fell −3.30% (FY2023) and −11.36% (FY2024) before recovering +20.18% (FY2025) [F_688347.md §1/§2.1], attributable net margin has been thin and volatile across the same window (11.93% → 2.65% → 2.18%) [F_688347.md §2.3], and top-5 customer concentration has risen every year (25.66% → 30.09% → 34.18%) [F_688347.md §3] — even as the CNY-basis gross margin this file's own Phase-2 extraction computes (18.72% FY2025) sits well above a separately-fetched, unresolved USD-basis figure (~11.78%) that F_688347.md flags as a genuine, unreconciled conflict rather than a simple FX effect [F_688347.md §2.2]. The task's own central question — is the A-share pricing the pending Huali (华力微) injection? — does not resolve cleanly to "yes." The disclosed acquisition price for 97.4988% of Huali is RMB 8.268bn (82.68亿元) [S914], only **≈1.47% of Hua Hong's own CNY563.02bn market cap** (8,268/563,023.35), so even a dramatic re-rating of Huali's standalone value could not, by itself, explain a multiple this extreme — the ~1141x figure is overwhelmingly a bet on Hua Hong's OWN existing, pre-Huali business re-rating, not an arithmetic effect of the deal's own disclosed size. Nor is it well explained as a bet on Huali's Ascend-linked upside specifically: the acquisition remains **not closed** as of this audit date (2026-07-07) — "unconditionally passed [SSE] review, pending only CSRC registration," expected H2 2026 [S913] — Huali's own disclosed 7nm/SiCarrier-linked line targets only "a few thousand wafers a month by the end of 2026" [S909], small next to Hua Hong's own existing >100,000-wafer/month-equivalent base [V_688347.md], and a named independent analytical outlet's own dated judgment is that Huali is "unlikely to meaningfully contribute to Chinese AI chip production for at least several years" [S911]; the one quantified, disclosed synergy from the deal itself is mature-node, not advanced-node — Huali's Fab 5 alone adds 38,000 wafers/month of 65/55nm-40nm capacity [S914] — and 688347's own CURRENT (FY2025/Q1-2026) consolidated revenue carries a verified **0% Huawei/Ascend exposure**, since Huali is not yet consolidated [V_688347.md]; the one AI customer Hua Hong has named on the record is Nvidia, not Huawei [S912]. What today's price more plausibly assumes, then, is a broader, structural re-rating of Hua Hong's entire existing mature-node/specialty-process base as a scarce domestic-semiconductor strategic asset — sustained >100% utilization [V_688347.md] converting into years of margin-accretive growth from a sub-3% net-margin base, continued 12-inch price hikes into 2026 [S910], and the FAB9A capacity plan's generically-named "AI power chip" 83,000 wafers/month [S913] ramping into real revenue — a China-self-sufficiency/scarcity premium rather than a precise, information-driven Ascend bet, a reading the A/H spread corroborates: 1347.HK trades at HKD180.000 [S1102], a CNY-equivalent of roughly CNY156.20 at a same-window Bank of China reference rate [S1105] — a ~+107% A-share premium per identical economic share even though H-shares are the majority (≈76.5%) of Hua Hong's total combined share count [S1102] — domestic capital paying more than double what internationally-accessible capital pays for the identical underlying cash flows, the same pattern this mission documented for SMIC [VAL_688981.md]. The price must also look past two disclosed headwinds specific to this name: a shrinking net-cash cushion (CNY24,963M → 16,660M → 12,824M, FY2023-25) amid heavy FAB9 (Wuxi) capex [F_688347.md §1/§2.4], and a U.S. BIS tool-shipment-restriction action naming "China No.2 Foundry Hua Hong" specifically (April 2026) [S909] — a real, dated, company-specific regulatory headwind independent of the revenue-exposure question.

**Flag: PRICED-FOR-PERFECTION** — a ~1141x trailing P/E (richer than any other dual-listed STAR-market foundry this mission has valued) against a sub-3% net margin and an acquisition (Huali) that is both still-unclosed and, at its own disclosed RMB8.268bn deal value, too small (≈1.5% of market cap) to explain the multiple on its own, while 688347's own current, consolidated Huawei/Ascend exposure is a verified 0% and Huali's prospective advanced-node contribution is independently judged "unlikely to meaningfully contribute...for at least several years" — together this prices in a multi-year, self-sufficiency-driven re-rating of Hua Hong's entire existing business that neither the FY2023-25 record nor the pending Huali deal's own disclosed scale yet supports.
