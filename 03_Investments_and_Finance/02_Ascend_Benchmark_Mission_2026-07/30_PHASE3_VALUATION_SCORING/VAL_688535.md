[S1130;S1131]), 688535 trades at ~746.31x TTM P/E (TTM attributable net profit CNY30.57M, built as

[S960;S961]) — or a materially lower ~25.60x on an annualized-Q1-2026 run-rate basis (Q1-2026 revenue

today's price the market has already conceded to the 衡所华威 consolidation reaching full annualized
scale. For even the CHEAPER of the two P/S readings — and for the P/E, which is extreme under every
basis computed — to be a fair price, the market must be underwriting at least two things this mission's
own Phase-1/Phase-2 work does not currently support. **First, on 衡所华威 integration:** the price
requires a clean, margin-accretive completion that reverses rather than repeats FY2025's own pattern,

itself [F_688535.md]. 衡所华威's own full-calendar-year FY2025 standalone figures (CNY480M revenue,
