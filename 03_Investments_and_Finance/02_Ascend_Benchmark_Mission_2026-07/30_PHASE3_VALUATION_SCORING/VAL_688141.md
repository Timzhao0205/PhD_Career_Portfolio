## Implied expectations

At CNY162.92/share and CNY73.37bn market cap (2026-07-07 post-close [S1110;S1111]), 杰华特/Joulwatt
trades at 27.63x FY2025 revenue (25.37x on a Q1-2026-rolled run-rate basis) with P/E undefined
(n/a) — the company has never posted a profitable fiscal year in this mission's Phase-1/2 record:
归母净利润 was -CNY531.41M (FY2023), -CNY603.37M (FY2024), -CNY717.12M (FY2025)
[S1028;S1027;S1026;S920], and the most recently reported quarter, Q1-2026, added another
-CNY276M loss, wider still (-CNY294M) excluding non-recurring items [S921;S923], at a computed
-36.08% net margin that is worse than FY2025's full-year -27.01% (no Q1-2025 comparator was
fetched in Phase 1/2 to confirm whether this is a genuine trend break or ordinary seasonality).
For a ~26–28x-sales multiple to be justified with zero trailing profit anywhere in the record, the
market must be underwriting a multi-year continuation of Joulwatt's genuinely strong revenue growth
(+58.15% FY2025, still +44.80% YoY in Q1-2026, though decelerating) for long enough that the
~36–38% of revenue plowed into R&D every year (CNY957.28M absolute in FY2025 alone, nearly double
FY2023's CNY499.37M) [S1026;S1027;S1028] eventually converts into GAAP profitability — a conversion
for which the directional evidence is genuinely mixed: net margin as a % of revenue DID improve for
three straight years (-40.98% → -35.94% → -27.01%) [S1028;S1027;S1026], but the underlying cash
economics moved the wrong way over the same window — operating cash flow was negative in all three
fiscal years (-291.43M/-360.44M/-323.50M) [S1022;S1021;S1020], capex nearly doubled
(CNY124.65M→216.00M) [S1022;S1020], and the balance sheet swung from +CNY632.25M net cash (FY2023)
to -CNY297.64M (FY2024) to -CNY1,814.50M net DEBT (FY2025) [S1025;S1024;S1023] — i.e. Joulwatt is
burning more real cash every year even as its loss narrows as a percentage of a fast-growing
revenue base, and Q1-2026 just interrupted the margin-improvement trend for the first time in the
record. Layered on top: a persistent, never-identified customer-concentration risk (top-5 customers
40–46% of revenue across 3 years, one anonymous #1 customer alone at 26–31% for the same 3 years
[S925]) whose only link to this mission's Huawei/Ascend hypothesis is a single named sell-side
report situating Joulwatt's DrMOS/multi-phase-controller products inside the Ascend supply chain
[S924] — Joulwatt's own FY2025 annual report text does not name Huawei or Ascend anywhere [S920],
and V_688141.md's tier-B ruling and NOT-ESTIMABLE exposure finding mean this mission cannot confirm
how much (if any) of the 58% revenue growth the market is extrapolating is actually AI/Ascend-linked
demand versus the DC-DC product line's other end-markets (53.29% of FY2025 revenue, spanning
PC/auto/industrial/comms) [S920]; and a pending H-share listing (resubmitted 2026-01-13, still in
draft/regulatory-review status [S928, snippet]) that would plausibly bring in cash the balance sheet
increasingly needs but would also dilute today's A-share holders on terms this mission's sources do
not quantify. Flag: **PRICED-FOR-PERFECTION** — a ~26–28x sales multiple with no earnings floor at
all, sitting on top of three consecutive years of widening absolute cash burn and a first-ever
net-debt position, prices in a profitability inflection this mission's own Phase-2 record has not
yet begun to show, while resting part of its growth narrative on an Ascend/Huawei linkage this
mission could verify only to tier B (not a confirmed anchor customer).
