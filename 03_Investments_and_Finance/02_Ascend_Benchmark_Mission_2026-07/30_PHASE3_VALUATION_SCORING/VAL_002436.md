## Implied expectations

At CNY42.23/share and CNY71.78bn total market cap (2026-07-07 post-close [S1120;S1121]), 兴森科技
trades at ~497.35x TTM P/E (TTM attributable net profit CNY144.32M, built as FY2025 CNY134.95M
[S940;S1040] − Q1-2025 CNY9.37M [S940] + Q1-2026 CNY18.74M [S1051;S950]) and ~9.66x TTM P/S (TTM
revenue CNY7,433.19M, same build from FY2025 CNY7,194.62M [S940;S1040], Q1-2025 CNY1,579.60M
[S940], Q1-2026 CNY1,818.17M [S1051]) — multiples that assume a materially stronger, more mature
company than the one Phase 1/2 verified. FY2025's actual print was a fragile, partial recovery,
not a breakout: gross margin rebuilt to only 19.57% [S1040] after collapsing from 23.32% (FY2023)
[S1042] to 15.87% (FY2024) [S1041]; 归母 net margin was just 1.88% [S940;S1040], and net margin on
a consolidated, pre-minority-interest basis was still NEGATIVE at -1.45% [S1040, derived] —
minority shareholders in loss-making subsidiaries are absorbing losses large enough that the
headline parent-attributable profit flatters the underlying business; operating cash flow swung
NEGATIVE for the first time in the 3-year window (-CNY62.75M, -116.70% YoY) [S940;S1046]; and net
debt widened every year (CNY2,182.70M→3,447.12M→4,354.62M, FY2023→FY2025) [S1045;S1044;S1043]
even as capex decelerated (CNY1,881.20M→1,127.87M→852.68M, same window) [S1048;S1047;S1046]. The
one growth vector that could justify a ~500x earnings multiple — FCBGA/ABF advanced-packaging
optionality into an AI-substrate, Huawei/Ascend-adjacent narrative — is the company's own admitted
laggard, not its current profit engine: its most recent, most comprehensive IR record (2026-05-08)
states plainly that "2023-2025年期间，公司FCBGA封装基板项目的整体经营不及预期，对公司净利润形成
较大拖累" (the FCBGA project has underperformed for three straight years, materially dragging down
profit), with FCBGA still in "小批量生产阶段" (small-batch production) and no mass/batch orders as
of that date [S941]; the Guangzhou FCBGA-scale-up subsidiary generated just 0.37% of FY2025
consolidated revenue while posting a large loss [S954;S955]. This is not a case of zero Huawei
relationship — the company separately confirmed, tier-A, that it cooperates with Huawei across
both its PCB and semiconductor/IC-substrate segments, dated 2023-09-08 [S942] — but that
confirmation is now three years stale, unquantified, and explicitly does not establish Ascend/
AI-chip specificity, the load-bearing element for a multiple this size. Directly asked, by name,
whether it supplies Huawei's Ascend 910C (2024-05-28) or Ascend 920 (2025-07-11) chips, the
company declined to confirm both times, offering only generic capability reassurances [S943;S944];
customer concentration has risen (top-5: 15.85%→25.62%→27.29%, FY2023→FY2025; #1 customer 13.06%
in FY2025) but no source names that customer, so the trend cannot be read as Huawei/Ascend-specific
either way [S948;S949]; and the specific, precise-sounding "70% of ABF capacity to Huawei Ascend,
CNY1.5bn+ of 2025 Huawei orders" narrative that circulates around this name traces, on direct
inspection, to an uncited tier-4 forum/self-media post, not to any filing, 互动易 reply, or named
analyst report [S945]. For today's price to be justified, the market must be underwriting a
FCBGA/ABF mass-production ramp — Huawei/Ascend-specific and large enough to turn a currently
sub-1%-of-revenue, loss-making line into the company's dominant profit driver within a few years —
a future the company's own most recent disclosures neither confirm nor project, and one that sits
uneasily against a business currently burning operating cash and adding net debt. Flag:
**PRICED-FOR-PERFECTION** — a ~497x trailing-earnings, ~10x trailing-sales multiple on 1.88% net
margins (negative on a consolidated basis), negative operating cash flow, and a
company-acknowledged three-year FCBGA underperformance prices in a Huawei/Ascend-scale
AI-substrate breakout that remains, per this mission's own Phase-1 audit, Tier-C/forum-sourced and
contradicted in several particulars by the company's own numbers.
