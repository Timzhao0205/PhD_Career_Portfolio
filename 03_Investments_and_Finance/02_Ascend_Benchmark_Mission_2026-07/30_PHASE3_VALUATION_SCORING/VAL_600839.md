## Implied expectations

At CNY 6.53/share (CNY 30.14bn total market cap), 600839 prices to ~30.5x FY2025 attributable earnings
and ~40.6x trailing-twelve-month earnings — the TTM multiple sits well above the FY2025-static one
specifically because Q1-2026 attributable net profit fell −71.49% YoY to ¥98.48M (F_600839.md §6),
shrinking the trailing earnings base even as the share price itself barely moved — and to only ~0.28x
sales on either basis. None of this looks like it prices the 华鲲振宇/Ascend story WATCHLIST.csv
originally flagged: V_600839.md's tier-A debunk (four separately-dated company denials, most recently
2026-05-13, "公司未从事昇腾AI服务器代工") puts 600839's DIRECT stake at 0% — the real 25%→3% stake sat
at the unlisted parent, 长虹控股集团, a distinct legal entity — and its own indirect look-through stake
at only ≈0.97% of a non-consolidated fund holding, which V_600839.md's own illustrative sanity check
sizes at ≈0.11% of 600839's FY2025 revenue even under a generous hypothetical treatment. The one REAL,
consolidated Huawei-linked channel — 长虹佳华 (60.13%-owned, rolling up into an ICT综合服务 segment that
is ≈37.35% of FY2025 revenue) — is general enterprise-IT distribution, not Ascend-chip-specific, and
carries the thinnest gross margin of 600839's eight reported segments (3.43% FY2025, F_600839.md §5),
consistent with reseller economics rather than a technology premium. What the multiple more plausibly
reflects instead is ordinary appliance-and-distribution-conglomerate economics, layered with a real but
easy-to-overstate profit-growth headline: group net margin is structurally thin (0.68–0.91% of revenue,
FY2023-25) and minority shareholders of partially-owned subsidiaries have historically claimed 50–62%
of TOTAL consolidated profit (F_600839.md §2.6) — the share 600839's OWN shareholders capture of that
total has been rising (38.25% FY2023 → 38.97% FY2024 → 49.68% FY2025), and continuation of that
mix-shift, more than any Ascend narrative, is the more defensible reading of FY2025's headline +40.56%
attributable-NP growth. That headline also needs a quality caveat the multiple may not be discounting:
FY2025's own ex-non-recurring attributable profit (扣非) fell −78.35% YoY in the SAME year headline
attributable NP rose +40.56% (F_600839.md §2.0), and Q1-2026 posted a similarly sized −78.09% 扣非
decline (F_600839.md §6) — in both the most recent full year and the most recent quarter, the
recurring/core-operations profit line moved sharply opposite to the more-visible headline
attributable-NP figure. Relative to genuine Ascend/AI-server-maker names (routinely priced far richer,
often on unprofitable or pre-revenue bases), 600839's 30–40x range looks modest — but that modesty
reflects the debunk being (correctly) unpriced AND the underlying appliance-conglomerate/thin-margin
discount simultaneously; the two explanations reinforce rather than compete with each other.

**Flag: NEUTRAL** — the ~30–41x P/E carries no 华鲲振宇/Ascend premium (correctly, given the verified
0% direct stake) and no distress discount for the just-reported Q1-2026 −71.49% profit decline or the
~78% swings in ex-non-recurring profit either, landing on an unremarkable conglomerate multiple that
has not yet resolved which recent print — FY2025's headline surge or Q1-2026's collapse — is the more
representative one.
