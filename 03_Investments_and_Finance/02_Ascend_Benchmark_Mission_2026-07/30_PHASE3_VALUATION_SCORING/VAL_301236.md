## Implied expectations

At CNY37.58/share and a CNY38.90bn market cap (2026-07-07 post-close [S663;S664]), 软通动力 trades
at an extreme, fragile ~719x TTM P/E (TTM attributable NP a razor-thin +CNY54.11M residual between
FY2025's +206.11M and two much larger, approximate quarterly losses) and ~188.7x on FY2025 NP
alone, while the more meaningful ~1.07x TTM P/S [S509;S295;S301;S303;S307] looks modest only
because the earnings base beneath it is so thin (FY2025 net margin 0.59%) and, on a
non-recurring-adjusted basis, actually shrinking (扣非归母净利润 fell -42.14% YoY to just
CNY41.93M even as headline NP rose +14.27% [S303] — roughly 79.6% of FY2025's already-small
headline profit is non-recurring government subsidies and fair-value gains, not operating earnings
power). For today's price to be justified on PROFIT — not just the revenue growth the company
keeps delivering (FY2025 revenue +12.05% YoY, Q1-2026 +15.79% YoY, "AI-related business" cited at
52.6% of FY2025 revenue [S295;S301;S303;S307]) — the market must be underwriting a multi-year
margin-and-cash inflection that has not yet shown up in any reported period: the Kunpeng/
Ascend-linked 计算产品与智能电子 hardware segment (bounded ≈19-45% of FY2025 revenue,
V_301236.md, derived from [S295;S301;S303] ceiling and [S299] floor-extrapolation) is growing fast
(+22.36% YoY) but still runs at only 4.82% gross margin versus 16.6-21.3% for the legacy software
business [S303;S304], a ≈12-16pp gap that has not narrowed across FY2023-FY2025 and is
structurally diluting consolidated margin as its revenue share rises; operating cash flow swung
negative for the first time in this 3-year window (FY2025 OCF -CNY242.03M [S515;S303], attributed
to advance component/inventory stocking ahead of hardware deliveries); and CNY208.08M of
acquisition goodwill from the 同方 deal [S512] sits on the balance sheet, tested as
low-impairment-risk only as of a Sept-2025 filing that predates the hardware segment's continued
margin decline through FY2025. None of these three lines — segment margin gap, OCF, core/
ex-non-recurring profit — improved in the most recently reported fiscal year; all three moved the
wrong way even as the headline growth narrative continued. Flag: **PRICED-FOR-PERFECTION** — a
fragile, near-zero trailing earnings base sitting under an extreme implied multiple, with
hardware-segment margins, operating cash flow, and non-recurring-adjusted profit all deteriorating
rather than improving in the latest reported year, prices in a Huawei-ecosystem
hardware-to-profit conversion that the record through Q1-2026 has not yet begun to demonstrate.
