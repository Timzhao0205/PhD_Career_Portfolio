**Terms (defined once, matching F_688629.md):** **gross margin** = (revenue − cost of revenue) /
revenue. **net margin** = net profit attributable to parent (归属于母公司所有者的净利润, "归母
净利润") / revenue — the "归母/state basis" per task instruction; this company shows negligible
minority interest all 3 years (consolidated 净利润 and 归母净利润 differ by ≤0.03万元 in every
year fetched), so no separate total-basis figure is tabulated. **OCF** (operating cash flow) =
经营活动产生的现金流量净额 (net cash from core operations). **capex** = 购建固定资产、无形资产
和其他长期资产支付的现金 (cash paid for fixed/intangible/other long-term assets), reported as a
positive spend magnitude. **net cash/(net debt)** = cash & equivalents − (short-term borrowings +
non-current liabilities due within 1 year + long-term borrowings + bonds payable); positive = net
cash, parenthesized negative = net debt — **same definition as F_688629.md**, applied identically
here (688519's 交易性金融资产/trading-financial-assets line is undisclosed, "--", in all 3 fiscal
years, so it is moot either way). **R&D intensity** = 研发费用 (P&L-expensed R&D) / revenue.
**top-5 customer concentration** = combined % of revenue from the 5 largest customers.
