## 4. Methodology note — data-source reliability this session

Primary numeric series (revenue, COGS, R&D expense, net profit, OCF, capex, balance-sheet debt/
cash components) were pulled from Sina Finance's static per-statement-type pages
(`vFD_ProfitStatement`/`vFD_BalanceSheet`/`vFD_CashFlow`, `stockid/600498/ctrl/<year>`), one fetch
per statement type per fiscal year (FY2022 base year included solely for the FY2023 YoY
calculation). Every load-bearing figure taken from these pages was cross-validated against
`V_600498.md`'s own P1-verified figures (revenue, net profit, revenue YoY%, net-profit YoY%,
short-term-borrowings YoY% — all matched exactly, §1/§2) and/or against East Money's
`datacenter-web.eastmoney.com` `RPT_F10_FINANCE_MAINFINADATA` JSON endpoint [S530] (gross margin,
net margin, OCF, revenue YoY — all matched) per this ticket's explicit instruction to
cross-validate aggregator numbers against P1 anchors before use. The one aggregator field found
NOT to match — East Money's "R&D expense" — is flagged and explained in §2.5 rather than silently
used or silently discarded.

Two additional endpoints were tried and discarded (no figure from either is used anywhere in this
file): (a) Sina's `vFD_FinancialGuideLine/stockid/600498/ctrl/2025` static page — the fetch
returned a table where "Revenue Growth Rate" and "ROE" columns held identical values
(2.49%/3.25%/2.04%/0.39% in both), an internally-implausible column-parsing artifact of the same
kind flagged for 10jqka in `F_000628.md`'s methodology note; this endpoint was abandoned in favor
of the ProfitStatement/BalanceSheet/CashFlow triad above. (b) `basic.10jqka.com.cn/600498/
finance.html` returned revenue and gross-margin figures that matched the Sina/East-Money-derived
figures in §1 exactly (e.g. 21.25%/20.56% gross margin, 285.49亿/311.30亿 revenue), but its "净利润"
(net profit) and "净利率" (net margin) figures for FY2023/FY2024 (¥4.55亿/3.84% and ¥6.53亿/5.04%
respectively) do NOT match either the attributable-basis (¥5.05亿/1.62%, ¥7.03亿/2.46%) or
total-basis (¥4.89亿/1.57%, ¥7.10亿/2.49%) net-profit figures independently confirmed via [S520]-
[S522] and [S530] — an unreconciled discrepancy of a different character than 10jqka's unit-
garbling problem documented for 000628, but similarly disqualifying; no 10jqka net-profit or
net-margin figure is used anywhere in this file.
