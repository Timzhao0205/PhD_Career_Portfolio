**Terms (defined once):** **gross margin** = (revenue − cost of revenue) / revenue. **net margin**
= net profit attributable to parent / revenue. **OCF** (operating cash flow) = cash generated by
core operations (经营活动产生的现金流量净额). **capex** = cash paid for fixed/intangible/other
long-term assets (购建固定资产、无形资产和其他长期资产支付的现金), a capital-investment proxy.
**net cash/(net debt)** = cash & equivalents − (short-term borrowings + non-current liabilities due
within 1 year + long-term borrowings + bonds payable); positive = net cash, parenthesized negative
= net debt. **R&D intensity** = R&D spend / revenue. **top-5 customer concentration** = combined %
of revenue from the 5 largest customers (customer/supply-chain risk metric).
