platforms report different bases without saying so:**

standard equity-analysis convention) and shows the total basis as a labeled alternate.

## Methodology note

This session's WebFetch calls on the cninfo-hosted PDFs [S145]/[S146]/[S147] again returned
"binary/corrupted, cannot extract" responses from WebFetch's own text-summarization step —
matching V_002261.md's documented Phase-1 finding. However, each WebFetch call also silently
saves a local copy of the fetched binary; **using the Read tool directly on that
locally-cached PDF path succeeded** for all three (7pp, 10pp, 3pp respectively — all within
the 20-page single-request limit), yielding full, exact tier-1 text neither WebFetch's model
nor V_002261.md's Phase-1 session had captured verbatim before. This let FY2023/24/25
revenue, both net-profit bases, OCF, total assets, and FY2025's full balance sheet be
upgraded from tier-3-estimated to tier-1-exact this session. The SAME technique was tried on
the much larger full FY2025 annual report (4.9MB, ~214pp, hosted at a sina mirror, not
cninfo) and failed differently: Read returned "PDF is password-protected." This is a harder
blocker than a missing page-rendering tool and is not evidently fixable within this
environment — customer/supplier-concentration and segment tables that live only in that
document remain MISSING (see log).
