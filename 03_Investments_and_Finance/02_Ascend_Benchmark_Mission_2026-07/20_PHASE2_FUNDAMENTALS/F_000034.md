### Gross margin
gm_pct = (revenue − cost) ÷ revenue
- FY2023: (11,962,388.77 − 11,485,401.14) ÷ 11,962,388.77 = 476,987.63 ÷ 11,962,388.77 =
  3.9877% ≈ **3.99%**. Cross-check: 同花顺F10 [S400] independently states 销售毛利率 3.99%
  for 2023-12-31 — exact match.
- FY2024: (12,816,639.26 − 12,276,955.13) ÷ 12,816,639.26 = 539,684.13 ÷ 12,816,639.26 =
  4.2108% ≈ **4.21%**. Cross-check: [S400] states 4.21% for 2024-12-31 — exact match.
- FY2025: (14,375,104.45 − 13,886,696.14) ÷ 14,375,104.45 = 488,408.31 ÷ 14,375,104.45 =
  3.3978% ≈ **3.40%**. Cross-check: a second, separately-prompted [S400] fetch returned a
  value labelled "2025-12-31" = 3.40% under a mislabeled field (see §C) — matches this
  computed figure exactly, corroborating it.

### Net margin
nm_pct = net profit attributable to parent ÷ revenue
- FY2023: 117,178.30 ÷ 11,962,388.77 = 0.97946% ≈ **0.98%**
- FY2024: 75,269.46 ÷ 12,816,639.26 = 0.58732% ≈ **0.59%**
- FY2025: 52,294.38 ÷ 14,375,104.45 = 0.36378% ≈ **0.36%**
- Cross-check: FY2025 vs FY2024 parent net profit = (52,294.38 − 75,269.46) ÷ 75,269.46 =
  −30.52% ≈ −30.5%, matching V_000034.md's already-verified [S100] statement that
  "consolidated net profit fell 30.5% YoY" — strong corroboration that 归母净利润 is the
  right numerator and that these absolute figures are correct.

### R&D intensity
rd_pct = 研发费用 (P&L-expensed R&D) ÷ revenue

- FY2025: 39,254.21 ÷ 14,375,104.45 = 0.27310% ≈ **0.27%**
- See §Methodology for why this (not the broader 研发投入 concept) is the basis used.

## Cross-checks / documented conflicts (per SOURCE_STANDARDS §5: record both values, prefer
the more internally-consistent one, do not average)

**§A — S100 (annual-report abstract PDF) unreadable this session for data tables.**
Two separate WebFetch attempts on `http://static.cninfo.com.cn/finalpage/2026-03-31/1225058635.PDF`
this session returned only PDF binary/FlateDecode-stream metadata, not extractable table
text — despite V_000034.md recording that the Phase-1 filing-auditor successfully read this
same PDF "in full (8pp)" for narrative prose. The likely explanation: the prose paragraphs
quoted in V_000034.md are true selectable text, while the *accounting data tables*
(3-year financial summary, R&D-investment table) in this PDF are rendered as embedded
images/vector graphics without a text layer — consistent with this mission's documented
KNOWN LIMITATION that full/abstract annual-report PDFs often fail to parse for tabular data
even when prose extracts. Resolution: all FY2023/FY2024/FY2025 revenue, cost, profit, R&D,
OCF, capex, and balance-sheet figures in this file are instead sourced from Sina Finance's
historical financial-statement static tables ([S401]–[S409]), which are cross-validated
against every S100-derived figure already in V_000034.md (FY2025 revenue, FY2025 OCF, FY2024
OCF, the −30.5% net-profit YoY, FY2025 top-5 customer %) with exact or near-exact matches —
treated as a faithful mirror of the filed statements for this ticker.

**§B — R&D "投入" vs "费用."** The mission's requested metric is phrased 研发投入占营业收入比例
(total R&D *investment* as % of revenue), which Chinese annual reports typically disclose in
a dedicated 研发投入情况表 that can exceed the P&L-expensed 研发费用 figure used above (the
gap is usually capitalized R&D). That dedicated table could not be retrieved this session (see
§A). One secondary, lower-confidence data point exists: 同花顺F10 经营分析 [S109] states
"2025年，公司研发支出4.15亿元" (415M CNY) with an odd "（预计）" (projected/estimated)
qualifier attached in the page's own framing — internally close to (8% above) this file's
computed FY2025 研发费用 of 392.54M CNY, which is directionally consistent with the R&D
expense figure being a subset of total R&D investment, but the "预计" framing means it is
not used as the primary, load-bearing figure here. **rd_pct in the CSV is 研发费用÷revenue,
a conservative lower-bound proxy for R&D intensity, not the broader 研发投入 concept.**

**§C — Aggregator "net margin" field rejected as unreliable.** Two separately-prompted
WebFetch calls on the identical URL `https://basic.10jqka.com.cn/000034/finance.html` [S400]
returned mutually inconsistent "销售净利率" (net margin) values: the first call reported
13.69% (FY2023) / 8.14% (FY2024); the second reported 3.99% / 4.21% / 3.40% for
FY2023/FY2024/FY2025 — which are identical to this file's independently-computed **gross**
margins, i.e. the second fetch appears to have mislabeled the gross-margin field as net
margin. Neither series reconciles arithmetically with [S400]'s own separately-and-consistently
-stated absolute net profit figures (7.53亿元 FY2024, 11.72亿元 FY2023, both of which DO match
this file's 归母净利润 inputs from [S402]/[S403] to the last digit) divided by revenue: e.g.
7.53亿 ÷ 128,166.39 (亿-scale check: 7.53 ÷ 1,281.66 = 0.587% ≈ 0.59%) — matching this file's
computed 0.59%, not [S400]'s stated 8.14%. Given this internal contradiction, [S400]'s own
"net margin" field is not used anywhere in this file; net margin is instead computed directly
from revenue and 归母净利润 (§ above), cross-validated via the independent −30.5% YoY check.

**§D — Top-5 customer concentration, FY2023 minor discrepancy.** A first, broadly-scoped
fetch of [S109] returned "38.99%" for FY2023; a second, narrowly-scoped fetch quoting the
page's raw text returned "前5名客户销售金额_467.06亿_元,占营业收入_39.04%". Since
467.06 ÷ 1,196.24 = 39.04% reconciles exactly by direct arithmetic, 39.04% is used and 38.99%
is treated as a transcription artifact of the first (non-verbatim) fetch.
